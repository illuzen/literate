Drugs that can cause hypertension 能引起高血壓的藥物

Translated by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu （in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

Drugs that can cause hypertension

  • Alcohol, amphetamines, ecstasy (MDMA and derivatives), and cocaine
  • Corticosteroids
  • Cyclosporine
  • Erythropoietin
  • Estrogens (including birth control pills) and other hormones
  • Many over-the-counter medications such as cough/cold and asthma medications -- particularly when the cough/cold medicine is taken with certain antidepressants like tranylcypromine or tricyclics
  • Migraine medications
  • Nasal decongestants
  • Ingestion of tyramine-rich foods in conjunction with monoamine oxidase inhibitors (MAOIs).

Source: http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/000155.htm

能引起高血壓的藥物:

 

•酒精，安非他命，搖頭丸（亞甲二氧基甲基安非他明及其衍生物）和可卡因
•皮質類固醇
•環孢素
•促紅細胞生成素
•雌激素（包括避孕藥）和其他激素
•許多過度服用非處方藥，如咳嗽/感冒和哮喘的藥物 - 尤其是如咳嗽/感冒藥同時服用某些抗抑鬱藥，如反苯環丙胺或三環素
•偏頭痛的藥物
•鼾粘膜充血消除药

  • 服用單胺氧化酶抑制劑（MAOIs）同時吃富含酪胺的食物。

Releasing Exterior 解表

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Releasing exterior: promoting sweating thus the toxin can be expelled and relieving the exterior syndrome.

Herbs for releasing exterior of wind cold syndrome: fang feng, ma huang,  zi su, sheng jiang (fresh ginger), xi xin,  cang er zi,  jiang huo,  bai zhi,  jin jie,  gui zhi, xin yi,  xiang ru (Elscholtzia patrini, Garche.),  hao ben,  cong bai (white part of green onion,Allium fistulosum L.),  hu sui, cheng liu (Tamarix chinensis).

Herbs for releasing exterior of wind heat syndrome: jiu hua,  bo he,  chai hu, ge gen, sang ye, sheng ma,  niu bang zi, man jing zi,  can rui,  dan dou chi, fu pin, mu zei.

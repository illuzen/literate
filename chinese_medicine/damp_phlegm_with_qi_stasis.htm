Damp Phlegm with Qi Stagnation 痰濕氣滯

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

"Damp phlegm with qi stasis " is a term describing the following syndrome: difficulty in swallowing, hot, tightness and pain in the chest area, nausea, vomiting, or spitting mucus, palpitation, dizziness, feeling heavy and tired, light color tongue with thin white fur, and slippery pulse.

痰濕氣滯之症狀：吞咽不利，胸膈灼痛，脘悶 , 惡心 , 嘔吐，或吐涎沫，心悸， 頭暈，身體沉困，舌淡苔薄白，脈滑。

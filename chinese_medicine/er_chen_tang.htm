er chen tang 二陳湯

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu                    按此看中文

Name of Formula: er chen tang ( he ji ju fang)

Uses: chronic bronchitis, chronic gastritis.

Prescription:

fu ling 19 g, chen pi 19 g, ban xia 12 g, gan cao 4 g, sheng jang 3 slices.

Bowl with two and a half bowls of water.

Drink an hour after meal while warm.

Note: The name of formula "er chen tang" literally means "two aged (ingredients) soup". The ingredients chen pi and ban xia are considered to be best when they have been aged for long time (chen).

An classical text mentioned that zhi ke, chen pi, ban xia, lang du,

ma huang, zhu yu should be aged (chen); then they are more effected.

Thus those six herbs mentioned above are called "liu chen", meaning

six aged (herbs). This formula "er chen" means "two aged (herbs)."

　

二陳湯 (和劑局方)

主治﹕胃炎。寒咳﹕濕痰咳嗽，痰多色白，胸膈痞悶，

惡心嘔吐，肢體困倦，頭暈心悸。

以上各證常見於慢性氣管炎。

　

處方﹕

　

茯苓  五 錢,  陳皮 四錢,  半夏 三 錢,  甘草 一錢 , 生薑 三片。

水 兩 碗 半 煎 八 分。 飯後一小時
溫服 。

方中半夏，陳皮属於所谓六陳中之两陳。以儲存陳久為佳。

有歌云：『枳 殼，陳皮，半夏齊，麻 黃，狼毒與茱萸，六般之藥宜陳久，入藥方知奏效奇。』

吴儀洛說：『《局方》陳皮 ，半夏貴其陳久，則少燥散之性，故名『二陳』』。（《成方切用》）其命名意義在於此。

註：二陳湯名稱资料來源：中山大學 West BBS-西子灣站 ccteng。

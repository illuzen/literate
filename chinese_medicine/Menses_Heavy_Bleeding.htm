Heavy Menstrual Bleeding (Menorrhagia) 月經過多  

Translated by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu          (in English and Chinese)

Formula for heavy bleeding

Formula I

Name of Formula:

Ping Gan Kai Yu Zhi Xue Fang (Rid of Depression and Arrest Bleeding Formula)

《source of formula: Fu Qing Zhu Gynaecology 》

Application: for liver qi stagnation, with heat in blood

Composition:

bai shao 30 g, bai zhu30 g, dang gui 30 g, dan pi 9 , san qi 9 g, sheng di huang 9 g, gan cao 6 g, jing jie 6 g, chai hu 3 g.
Boil with water. One dose per day.

Formula II

Composition:

ai ye 2 qian,  dang gui 3 qian, bai shao 3 qian, sheng_di huang 3 qian, chuan xiong 1 qian.  

Note: 1 qian = 3.75 g (old measurement)

Boil with three bowls of water. Simmer till one bowl. Make into 3 servings. Use as tea.

 

月經過多

處方一：

中藥處方（一）

藥方名：平肝開鬱止血湯《方源：傅青主女科 》  卷上
 

主治：肝氣鬱結， 血熱

組成：醋炒白芍30克，土炒白朮30克，酒洗當歸30克，牡丹皮 9克，三七末9克，酒炒生地黃9克，甘草 6克，荊芥6克，柴胡3克。

水煎服，每日1劑，日服2次。

 

處方二﹕

艾葉二錢﹐ 當歸三錢﹐白芍三錢﹐生地黃三錢﹐ 川芎一錢。

加水三碗。煎成一碗。分三次服。

lecithin       

edited by Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Phospholipids of egg yolk. A mixture of phosphatidyl choline and phosphatidyl ethanolamine, but usually refers to phosphatidyl choline. 

Any phospholipid that consists the following:

CH2 OR1 CHOR 2 CH2 OPO2 OHR3. 

R1 R 2are lipids. R 3  is choline.

It is an emulsifier and an anti oxidant.

chun gen pi 椿根皮

 

Edited by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Ailanthi Radicis Cortex

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Ailanthus altissima (Mill.) Swingle

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

chinpi

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

ch'unp'i

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

ceon1 pei4

Other Names:

別名﹕

chun gen bai pi, chun bai pi, chun pi.

椿根白 皮，椿白皮，椿皮。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

bark or root bark of ailanthus

Distribution:

產地﹕

Shandong, Liaoning, Henan, Anhui

山東，遼寧，河南， 安徽。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

bitter, tart, cold.

味苦，涩，性寒

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

large intestine, liver, stomach

大腸﹐胃﹐ 肝。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

  1. antibiotic
  2. inhibits ulcers
  3. anti-cancer
  4. rids of round worms

 

  1. 抗菌作用
  2. 抑制潰瘍
  3. 抗癌
  4. 治蛔虫

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

uterus bleeding, post partum bleeding,

excessive bleeding not during menses,

yeast or bacterial vaginal infection, gonorrhea,

spermatorrhea, dysentery with blood.

子宮出血，產後出血，月經過多，血崩，赤白帶，

淋病，夢遺滑精，赤痢。

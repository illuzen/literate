wan nian hao 萬年蒿

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 Herba Artemisiae

Botanical name:

學名﹕

 Artemisia sacrorum Ledeb

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 iwayomogi

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 deo-wi-ji-gi

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

 maan6 nin4 hou1

Other Names:

別名﹕
wan nian hao (by Chinese Medical Journal) 萬年蒿(中藥誌)
yin chen hao (in Heilongjiang province) 茵陳蒿 (黑龍江)
bai lian hao (in Jiangsu province) 白蓮蒿 (江蘇)
tie gan hao (in Beijing) 鐵桿蒿 (北京)

Common Name:

英文名﹕

  

Distribution:

產地﹕

 Except cold and high altitude areas, it almost can be found in all China. Also it is distributed in Japan, Korea, Mongolia, Afghanistan, India, Pakistan, Nepal, Russia, Kashmir etc.

除高寒地區外，幾遍布全中國；日本，朝鮮，蒙古，阿富汗，印度，巴基斯坦，尼泊爾，俄羅斯，克什米爾地區也有。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 bitter, acrid, neutral.

苦，辛，平。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕
Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

  

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

 Being used in hepatitis, appendicitis, children epilepsy, yinxu with hot flashes. External use in injuries and bleeding.

清熱解毒，涼血止痛。用於肝炎，闌尾炎，小兒驚風，陰虛潮熱；外用治創傷出血。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

  

Dosage:

用量﹕

 Taking orally: 9 to 12 g.

External application: use appropriate amount. Use fresh or dry herb. Smash or grind into powder and apply to affected area.

内服： 9 ～ 12 克。外用適量，鮮品搗爛敷或乾品研粉撒患處。

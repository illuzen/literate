shan nai  山奈

　

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Rhizoma Kaempferiae galangae

Biological name:

學名﹕

Kaempferia galanga Linn

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

san na (山奈); banukon  (蕃鬱金)

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

saeng gan （沙薑）

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

saa1 goeng1 (sha jiang  沙薑.),

Other Names:

別名﹕

shan nai 山奈.﹐san nai  三奈  ﹐san nai zi  三奈子﹐sha jiang  沙薑.，fan yu jin 蕃鬱金。

Common Name:

外文名﹕

English:aromatic ginger, resurrection lily

Indonesian: kencur

Thai: proh horm, waan horm

Distribution:

產地﹕

Southeast Asia and southern parts of China.

分佈東南亞與華南各地。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

acrid, warm.

味辛，性溫。

Channels (meridians) entered:

　

歸經﹕

stomach

胃經。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

　

The active ingredient kaempferol:
  1. antibiotic effect: inhibits  Staphylococcus aureus, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, Salmonella typhi, Lactobacillus sp
  2. antifungus: Candida Albicans [1]
  3.  inhibits aldose reductase of eyes thus helps the treatment of the cataract of diabetic patients.
  4. arrest  cough; for treatment of bronchitis
  5. antimutagenic  properties:at concentration of 1×10-4mol/L, it inhibits the production of lymphatic cells.
  6. anti-inflammatory effect by inhibiting the production of nitric oxide [2]

山奈酚具有：

  1. 抗細菌：對黃色葡萄球菌及綠膿杆菌，傷寒杆菌，痢疾杆菌均有抑制作用。
  2. 抗霉菌作用：對抗白色念珠球菌有效。[1]
  3. 止咳：治療支氣管炎。
  4. 抑酶：抑制眼醛糖還原酶，有利於糖尿病白內障的治療。
  5. 具有誘變劑活性，當濃度為1×10-4mol/L時，可抑制淋巴細胞增殖。
  6. 抑制一氧化氮之生成而抑制發炎。[2]

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

chest and abdomen paint, dysentery, tooth cavity and ache. Also use as spice in cooking.

Today it is being used in anti cancer, anti-pregnancy, anti epilepsy, anti inflammation, anti spasm, anti ulcer, promoting bile secretion, as diuretics, arresting cough.

心腹冷痛 ， 寒濕霍亂 ， 風蟲牙痛 。 亦作料理香料。

現用於抗癌，抑制生育，抗癲癇，抗炎，作抗氧化劑，解痙，抗潰瘍，利膽利尿劑，止咳。

Dosage:

用量﹕

10  to 20 g. Concentrated extract: 0.5 to 2 g.

10∼20克；濃縮中藥：0.5∼2克。

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

For treatment of pain and cold abdomen: shan nei, ding xiang, dang gui, gan cao of equal portions; grind into powder and make into pills, size of soy beans. Take 39 pills each time with rice wine. (Pin Hu Ji Jian Fang)

治心腹冷痛：山柰，丁香，當 歸，甘草等分。為末，醋糊丸，梧子大(如黄豆大)。每服三十丸，酒下。（《瀕湖集簡方》

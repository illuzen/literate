Er Bai Point 二白穴

Translated by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Location: 4 cun above the wrist crease, proximal to PC7  (da ling point), on both sides of the flexor carpi radialis tendon.

There are 2 points on each arm. They are points outside the meridians (channels).

Application:
 hemorrhoids , anal itching, and rectal prolapse.

定位: 在腕掌横纹的中點處上4寸，當掌長肌腱與橈侧腕屈肌腱之間。 靠近大陵 (PC 7)。每條手臂有兩個穴位。是經外穴道。

主治：痔瘡，肛門發癢，脫肛。

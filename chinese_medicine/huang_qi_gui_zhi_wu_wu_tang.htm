huang qi gui zhi wu wu tang  黄芪桂枝五物湯
 

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu    按此看中文  （in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

Name of Formula: huang qi gui zhi wu wu tang （Source of formula:Jin Gui)

Application:

Peripheral neuropathy, sequelae of strokes, arthritis, sciatica, Raynaud's syndrome, scleroderma, myocarditis, bedsores, angioedema (angioneurotic edema, Quincke's edema ) caused by qixu (qi deficient) and blood deficient with infection of the blood vessels. cerebral palsy [2].

Prescription:

huang qi 18 g, bai shao 10 g, gui zhi,  10 g, sheng jiang 12 g, da zao   6 pieces

Boil with water. Use as tea.

Adjustment to formula:

  • If qixu (qi deficient) is serious, change huang qi to 30 g, and add dang shen 18 g.

  • If xue xu (blood deficient) is serious, add  ji xue teng 18 g﹐dang gui 12 g

  • If syndrome of cold or flu is obvious, add  fang feng12 g﹐jing jie 12 g

  • If the cold or flu is accompanied with plegm, add fa ban xia12 g﹐bai jie zi 10 g, bai fu zi 8 g.

  • If numbness is serious, add quan xie 4 g﹐jiang can 8 g

 [功 用] 益氣和 營，温經通痹。

[主治] 血痹証。症见肌膚麻木不仁 ，脉微涩而緊 者。

[藥方組成] 黃耆18 克﹐白 芍 10 克﹐桂 枝 10 克﹐生 姜 10 克﹐大 棗 6枚

[臨床應用] 周圍神經炎，腦中風後 遺症，關節炎，坐骨神經痛，雷諾氏病，硬皮病，心肌炎，褥瘡，血管 神經性 水腫等属于營衛氣 虛, 血 虛， 復感風邪入侵血脉者，腦性麻痺症。

[加减法]

Note:

Dopamine agonists stimulate the receptors in nerves in the brain that normally would be stimulated by dopamine. Unlike levodopa, a dopamine agonist is not changed (converted) into dopamine when it enters the body, but it behaves like dopamine.

  • Benzodiazepine works by decreasing abnormal electrical activity in the brain.

  • Carbamazepine is used alone or in combination with other medications to control certain types of seizures.

  • Pergolide msylate (Permax) has been withdrawn from the U.S. market since March 29, 2007 due to the possibility of heart valve damage.  It is used to treat Parkinson's disease, hyperprolactinemia, stiffness, tremors, spasms, and poor muscle control,and restless leg syndrome.

ulcerated chronic colitis     潰瘍性慢性結腸炎

 

Edited by: Joe Hing kwok Chu （in Chinese and English 中英文）

 

Name of Formula: kui yang tang (decoction for ulcers)

Application: ulcerated chronic colitis

Use jiu xiang chong as main ingredient, add bu gu zhi, wu wei zi, bai zhu, san qi, rou dou kou. Make into decoction. Effective rate was 87%.

 

(Source: Shaanxi Traditional Chinese Medicine, 1999, 4: 168)

 

潰瘍性慢性結腸炎

 

藥方名：潰瘍湯

 

九香蟲，加補 骨脂，五 味子，白朮，三 七，肉荳蔻。

 

其療效顯著，總有效率高達87％（陝西中醫，1999，4：168）。

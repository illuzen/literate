da zhui point  DU14, GV14, 大椎穴  (in English and Chinese 中英文)

Compiled by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu  朱興國編譯   

da zhui point DU 14, GV14

Location: In the mid-line of the back, in the depression below the seventh cervical vertibra. The 7th cervical vertebra is the large vertebra that stick out when the patient tilt the head forward.

Indications: exogenous disease, progressive heat consumption, mania epilepsy and other diseases of the mind, stiff neck, spinal pain, urticaria, acne etc.

大椎穴

定位：在背部正中線上，第7頸椎棘突下凹陷中。

主治：外感病症，骨蒸潮熱，癲狂癇症等神志病，項強，脊痛，風疹，痤瘡等。

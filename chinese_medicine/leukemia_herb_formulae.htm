Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) for Leukemia

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu     按 此 看 中 文

The following formulae are commonly used in leukemia. (Source: Chinese Medicine in Tumor Study 中醫癌腫學)

The formulae:

  1. ping xiao san 平消散﹐

  2. bu jin wan 補金丸

  3. shen lou san 參樓散,

  4. shen zhu wan 參朮丸,

  5. zhong shen wan 重參丸;

Use any of the above formulae on early stages and take it together with dan fan san 蛋礬散

Warning: formulae that contain xiong huang (realgar) only suitable for cases of high white cell counts.

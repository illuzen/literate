To Find Answer to Your Health Questions:     

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Click on the item "Search This Site" on the left side of this page or at the bottom of most pages. When the search form appears, type in the item that you are looking for. If you cannot find the information, you can email our research librarian . 

To use the services of the research librarian, there will be a charge of $25 per item per page of research. The fee is payable with a credit card to the librarian .

If you need chemical ingredients of certain herbs in a hurry or you need the bibliography on certain information, there is a set up cost of US$150 plus US$25 per page because most reference sources are in Chinese and Chinese is more difficult to type and to translate into English.

The fees charged by the librarian are subject to change. You can get a quote from the librarian  directly.

yi huang san tang 易黃散湯

 

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu             按此 看中文

 

Name of Formula: yi huang san tang

 

Application:

For vaginal discharge due to yeast infection or bacterial infection with pixu (spleen deficient) condition.

 

Prescription:

qian shi 20 g, shan yao 12 g, bai guo 12 g (smashed), huang bai 9 g, che qian zi 9 g.

 

Add water and make into decoction. Use as tea.

Adjust the weight of herbs according to diagnosis.


藥方名：易黃散湯

 

主治﹕脾虛﹐細菌或黴菌感染而致黃白帶

 

處方：芡實 20 克﹐山藥 12 克﹐白果 12克打碎﹐黃粕 9克﹐車前子 9克。

水煎服。

 

藥方之份量﹐須隨證加減。

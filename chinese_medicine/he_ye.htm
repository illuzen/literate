he ye 荷葉

 

Edited by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Folium Nelumbinis Nuciferae

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Nelumbo Nucifera Gaertn.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

kayo

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

hayop

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

ho4 yip6

Other Names:

別名﹕

xia 遐 ，lian ye 蓮葉.

 

Common Name:

英文名﹕

lotus leaves

Distribution:

產地﹕

throughout China

中國各地。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

bitter, slightly sweet, neutral

苦﹐微甜﹐平。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

heart, liver, spleen.

心﹐肝﹐ 脾。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

for:
  1. fever, irritability, excessive sweating, scanty urine, diarrhea due to summer heat
  2. edema,
  3. dizziness,
  4. diarrhea due to pixu,
  5. stops bleeding in the urinary tract.
  6. nose bleeding,
  7. vomiting bleed
  8. hypertension.

主治﹕

  1. 暑濕泄瀉，煩躁﹐尿少﹐出汗多
  2. 水氣浮腫
  3. 眩暈，
  4. 脾虛泄瀉
  5. 尿道出血
  6. 衄血，
  7. 吐血﹐
  8. 血壓高。

 

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

 

Dosage:

用量﹕

9 ~ 15 g. Can be up to 30 g when being used by itself or with a few herbs.

處方舉例﹕

毒素與禁忌﹕

not to use in cases of bleeding due to cold or deficiency. No compatible with fu ling.

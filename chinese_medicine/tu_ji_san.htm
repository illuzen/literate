tu ji san  禿 雞 散

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu    按此看中文

Name of formula: tu ji san

Uses: shenxu (kidney deficient) basic tonic formula.

Composition:

rou cong rong  210 g, she chuang zi 90 g,

yuan zhi  40 g, wu wei zi  15 g, tu si zi 120 g,

Wash the herbs and bake till dry. Grind into powder.

Take 12 g each time, twice a day, with lightly

salted drinking water.

Adjustment:

For those with shenyangxu (kidney yang deficient):

add: rou gui,  fu zi,  lu rong, ba ji tian,  zi he che,  dong chong xia cao

Also see ming men lacks fire.

For those with shenyinxu (kidney yin deficient): add shu di huang,  gui ban,   zhi mu, 

huang bai,  nu zhen zi,   han lian cao

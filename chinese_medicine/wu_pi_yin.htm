wu pi yin 五皮飲

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu                  按此看中文

Name of Formula: wu pi yin

Application: edema of cardiac, spleen or kidney source, difficulty in urination, short of breath, flatulence, edema during pregnancy.

Composition: 

wu jia pi, di gu pi, fu ling pi, du fu pi 15 g each, sheng jiang pi 8 g.

Make into decoction. Drink as tea, any time.

Adjustment to formula:

1.face is puffy: add chen pi, sang bai pi
2.flatulence in the waist and abdomen: add bai zhu,  cang zhu
3.edema in knees and feet: add zhi mu,  huang bai.

 

五皮飲 (醫方集解)
 

主治：

心臟，脾臟，腎臟性之水腫，面目虛浮，四肢脹滿，小便不利，氣喘腹脹症等。妊娠胎水皮水。
 

組成：

五加皮，地骨皮，茯苓皮，大腹皮 各15克，生薑皮 8 克 。

水煎。不拘時溫服。


加減：
1.面目虛浮：加陳 皮，桑白皮。
2.腰腹脹滿：加白 朮，蒼朮。
3.膝足水腫：加知母，黃 柏  。

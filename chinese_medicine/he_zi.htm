he zi 訶子     (in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Fructus Terminaliae Chebulae

Biological name:

拉丁文學名﹕

Terminalia chebula Retz.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

kashi

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

hacha

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

ho1 zi2

Other Names:

別名 ﹕

he li 訶黎 、he li le 訶黎勒、sui feng zi 随風子

Common Name:

英文名﹕

Medicine Terminalia Fruit, myrobalan fruit, chebula, chebgium

Distribution:

產地﹕

Guangdong, Yunnan, Guangxi. Originally produced in India and Burma areas.

分佈於廣東﹐雲南﹐廣西。原 產印度、緬甸等地

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 bitter, sour, astringent, neutral

 性平，味苦、酸 、涩。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

  1. anti bacteria

  2. improves cardiac function

  3. anti spasm of smooth muscles

  4. inhibits cancer growth

 

  1. 抗 菌

  2. 增 強 心 臟 機 能

  3. 抗 平 滑 肌 之 痙攣

  4. 抑 制 腫 瘤 之 生 長

Channels (meridians) entered:

歸經﹕

lung, stomach, large intestine

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

tighten intestine and stop chronic diarrhea, stops coughing, lessen inflammation of the throat, treats loss of voice,  improves eyesight, for inflammation of the eye lids, edema, ascites with varicose vein, blood in stool, prolapse of the rectum, asthma due to lung deficient, chronic cough, sore throat with coarse voice. .

涩腸止 瀉 ﹐  斂肺，降火利咽﹐ 治 失 聲﹐  眼睑糜爛，有增强視力，消腫之功效。用于暴發火眼，眼 翳，羞明，頭痛流泪，腹水青筋暴露﹐水 腫 , 久瀉久痢, 便血脱肛、肺虚喘咳﹐久咳不止﹐咽痛音啞。

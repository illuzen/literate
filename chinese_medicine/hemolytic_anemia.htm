hemolytic anemia

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu     按此看中文

Anemia resulting from the dissolution or destruction of red blood cells

Causes of hemolytic anemia:

  1. autoimmune dysfunction

  2. physical injuries

  3. other causes

Chinese herbs that can help produce red cells:

he shou wu, e jiao, dang gui, ji xue teng, shu di huang etc.

Avoid using these herbs: jin yin hua , ma huang, huang lian, borneol, la mei hua,  also vitamin K, broad bean (Vicia faba)

 jiao san xian 焦 三仙

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu  

Name of Formula: jiao san xian   按此看中文。

Application:

lack of appetite, weak digestion, flatulence, burping with rotten smell, nausea, vomiting, irregular bowel movements, may have low fever, anxious, tongue with thick, white and oily fur.

Note: this formula can be use in sequela caused by binging{1]. Also see: this for yeast infection from over eating carbohydrates.

Composition:

roasted shan zha, roasted shen qu, mai ya 15 g each.

Make into decoction. One dosage per day.

Note: do not overheat mai ya or it may lose its contend of amylase which is an important ingredient for promotig digestion.

方名： 焦 三仙

主治﹕

不思飲食﹐食而不化﹐腹部脹滿﹐噯腐吞酸﹐惡心嘔吐﹐大便失常，或有低熱﹐煩躁不安﹐舌苔白，厚 ，膩等症狀。
組成：

焦山楂 15 克﹐ 焦神麴 15 克﹐ 麥芽  15克 。

水煎飲服。每日一劑。

註：麥芽不可過度加熱以免降低其澱粉酶的作用。

按此 看念珠球菌感染治療法。

 

[1] from observation of the editor.

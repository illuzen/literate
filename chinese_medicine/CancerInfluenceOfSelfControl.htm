INFLUENCE OF SELF-CONTROL QIGONG TREATMENT ON THE CELL IMMUNE FUNCTION OF CANCER PATIENTS

 Zhang Longmin, Wan Wiuqung (Beijing Miyun Jinghua Cancer Hospital)

 Yu Yi, Ge Yanlu et al (Clinical Scientific Research Institution, Sino-Japanese Friendship Hospital)

 Through the joint co-operation and clinical practice of Sino-Japanese Friendship Hospital and our hospital, we have studied the influence of self-control treatment on the cell immune function of cancer patients by using the immunology and cell method. The patients include both primary and metastasized cancer patients. Among them were patients with lung cancer (43 cases), liver cancer (10 cases), stomach cancer (22 cases), intestinal cancer (11 cases), mammary cancer (8 cases) and other cancers (12 cases). By comparing the results pre-and post-one course of self-control treatment, we found that there were changes in red blood cells, lymphocytes, neutrophilic granulocyte immunity, the amount of white blood cells in peripheral blood diagram of cases with radiochemical treatment. All the experimental results have statistical significance. 

I. C3b receptor's floral loop rate which represents red blood cell immune function: pre-treatment: X+-SD=8. 40+-4. 68; post treatment: X+-SD=10.95+-4.68, post-treatment: X+-SD=6. 41+-2.78, p<0.001, showing patient's RBC immunization adherent function and the function of clearing up prominently immunizations mixture have been strengthened. 

II. The reversion rate of lymphocytes which represents body's immunity: pre-treatment: X+_=54.35+-14.94, post-treatment:

X+-SE=64.55+_10.31, p <0.01, an increase by 20.07%, showing T lymphocytes' immune function has been increased prominently. 

III. Changes of neutrophilic granulocyte's immune function:(1) moving function." The moving function of patients' neutrophilic granulocyte has been increased greatly after the self-control treatment; pre-treatment moving distance; 1.75+-0.53mm while post-treatment: 2.35+-0.77mm, p<0.001. (2) changes of phagocytosis function: the average, phagocytosis: pre-treatment:32.5+_9.22, post-treatment:51.33+-12.24, p<0.01, which shows the phagocytosis function of neutrophilic granulocyle has been strengthened. IV. bacteriocidal function of neutrophilic granulocyle has become strengthened prominently. 

IV. The influence of the self-control treatment on WBC number of patients before and after radiochemical treatment: experimental group: 2d-6362 before treatment, 2d=3950 after treatment, the difference: 3580+-188.3. T=8. 71, p (0.001). This shows that self-control treatment can regulate blood diagram, strengthen the immune function and reduce the by-effects of radiochemical treatment. V. Inducing interferons: The increase of inducting interferons after treatment shows the strengthening of patients' immunity, T-lymphocytes ability of endocrine lympho factors of patients' immunity.

               The above results and clinical curative effect are positively related. Patients' symptoms are relieved. The condition becomes stable. The size of the tumor shrank. The tumor may disappear at last. All these show that the self-control treatment is very effective in improving the immunity of patients and has a unique function in the comprehensive and  treatment and recovery.

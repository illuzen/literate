Food High in Oxalic Acid 高草酸的食物

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu  (in Chinese and English 中英文）

People who have diabetes, kidney disease, gout, rheumatoid arthritis or chronic pain in the vulva should avoid food high in

oxalic acid.

  1. buckwheat 喬麥

  2. star fruit (Averrhoa carambola) 楊桃

  3. black pepper 黑胡椒

  4. amaranth 莧菜

  5. celery 芹菜

  6. parsley 洋芫荽

Editor note:

*Leaves of beets are high in oxalic acid so as beets. Although beets are high in oxalic acid but beets are high in minerals which may inhibit the absolving of oxalic acid by the body.

编者註：甜菜根與甜菜葉都含有高草酸，但在甜菜根也含有高礦物質而可能抑制或避免草酸被人體吸收。

有一下情形者須避免吃以上高草酸之食物：

糖尿病，腎臟病，痛風，風濕，外陰部觸覺痛 (vulvodynia)等 。

預防結石須多喝水。如果尿 色太深，如無發熱，或食物，藥物 影響便可能是飲水不足。但過度喝水也有害。

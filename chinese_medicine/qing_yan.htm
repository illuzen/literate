da qing yan 大青鹽    (in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

halitum

Botanical name:

拉丁生物學名﹕

halitum

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

gan'en  (岩塩)

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

am yeon

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

cing1 jim4

Other Names:

別名﹕

rong yan

戎鹽

hu yan

胡鹽

tu deng yan

禿登鹽

yin tu yan

陰土鹽

shi yan

石鹽

羌鹽

qing yan

青鹽

yan yan

岩鹽

da qing yan

大青鹽

Common Name:

外文名﹕

halite

Distribution:

產地﹕

Qing hai, shaanxi, Shandong, Anhui, Yunnan, Gansu, Xinjiang, Neimenggu (inner Mongolia).

主產於 青海，而其他省份如陝西、山東、安徽、雲南、甘肅、新疆、內蒙古也産。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

salty, cold.

鹹，寒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

heart, liver, lung, kidney, bladder.

心，肝，肺 ，腎，膀胱。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

for treatment of blood in urine, spitting blood, gum and tongue bleeding, swollen red eyes,  blepharitis, toothache, constipation.

 

主尿血；吐血；齒 舌出血；目赤腫痛；眼瞼發炎；牙痛；大便秘結
研末揩牙；或水化漱口、洗目。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

Mainly contains sodium chloride, and other impurities like potassium chloride, magnesium chloride, calcium chloride, magnesium sulfate, calcium sulfate, iron.

主含氯化鈉(NaCl)。還 雜質如氯化 鉀(KCl)、氯化鎂(MgCl2)、氯化鈣(CaCl2)、硫酸鎂(MgSO4)、硫酸鈣(CaSO4)和鐵(Fe)等。

Daly Dosage:

每日用量﹕

oral: 0.9～1.5 g in decoction, or make into pills, or powder. External use: use appropriate amount.

內服：煎湯，0.9-1.5g；或入丸、散。外用：適量，

毒素與禁忌﹕

Not to be used in case of edema or vomiting.

水腫忌服，嘔吐者禁用。

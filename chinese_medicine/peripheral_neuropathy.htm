Peripheral Neuropathy  周 邊神經病變  (in English and Chinese 中英文)
 

Translated and edited by: Joe Hing kwok Chu     按此看中文

 

Nervous system can be classified into 2 groups:

  1. Central nervous system: brain and spinal cord.
     

  2. Peripheral nerves: all the other nerves. Causes of nerve peripheral neuropathy, include:

    * sensory nerves: sensory, such as heat, pain or touch
    * motor nerves: control muscle movement
    * Automatic nerves: control of autonomic functions such as blood pressure, heart rate, digestion and bladder function
     


    Causes of peripheral neuropathy : nerve damage. Causes of nerve damage can be trauma, infection, systemic or metabolic problems and exposure to toxins.

    Some of the most common causes of systemic and metabolic problems are:

    diabetes

    cancer
    nutritional deficiencies: such as vitamin B12, iron, folic acid
    excessive alcohol consumption
    multiple myeloma
    uremia (kidney failure)

See: huang qi gui zhi wu wu tang 黃耆桂枝五物湯

 

周邊神經病變

 

神經系統分為兩大類：

  1. 中樞神經系統：大腦和脊髓。

  2. 周邊神經：即所有其他神經。

  •  感覺神經：感覺如熱，痛或觸摸

  •  運動神經：控制肌肉移動

  •   自主神經的控制的自動功能如血壓，心率，消化和膀胱功能

 

造成周邊神經病變的原因：神 經損傷。 導致 神 經損傷可能是 外傷，感染，系统性或代謝性問题和接觸的毒素。

 

其中最常見的系 统性與代謝問題的原因有：

  • 糖尿病

  • 癌症

  • 营養缺乏：如維生素B12，鐵質，葉酸

  • 飲酒過多

  • 多發性骨髓瘤

  • 尿毒症(腎 功能衰竭)

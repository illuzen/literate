jue ming zi 決明子；cao jue ming 草決明

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu        (in English and Chinese 中英文)

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Semen Cassiae

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Cassia tora, L.; Cassia obtusifolia L

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

ketsumeishi

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

kyolmyongja

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

kyut3 ming4 zi2

Other Names:

別名﹕

cao jue ming 草决明，jue ming zi 決明子，ma ti jue ming 馬蹄決明，ma ti cao 馬蹄草，jia lu dou 假綠豆。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

cassia seed

Distribution:

產地﹕

throughout China, especially in Anhui, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Guangdong, Guangxi, Sichuan.

全國均産，尤其在安徽，江蘇，浙江，廣東，廣西，四川等省 。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

salty, neutral.

鹹，平。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

liver, kidney, large intestine.

入肝，腎 ，大腸。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

for treatment of amaurosis, cataract, conjunctivitis, red, swollen painful eyes, headache, chronic constipation, hypertension, liver diseases.

用於肝膽郁熱所致的目赤腫痛，怕光流淚，頭痛。

治青盲內障，結膜炎，目赤腫痛，多淚羞明，頭痛頭脹，慢性便秘，高血壓，肝臟疾患。

Dosage:

用量﹕

10～15克。

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

Acute conjunctivitis: cao jue ming,  ju hua, huang qi. Add water and make into decoction and use as tea.

Hypertension: cao jue ming, ju hua, xia ku cao.  Add water and make into decoction and use as tea.

急性結膜炎: 草决明，菊 花，黃 芩。水煎服 。

高血壓：草决明，菊 花，夏枯 草。水煎服。

dang gui nian tong tang 當歸拈痛湯   (in English and Chinese)

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu      按此看 中文

Name of Formula: dang gui nian tong tang (Li Dong Yuan)

Uses: arthritis of the limbs, beriberi, skin diseases of the lower limbs and groin area, gonorrhea.

Prescription:

yin chen hao 12 g, qiang huo 8 g, dang gui 12 g, zhi mu 12 g, ze xie12 g,

zhu ling 12 g, bai zhu 12 g, huang qin 8 g, ge gen  12 g, fang feng  8 g,

ren shen 8 g, cang zhu   8 g, gan cao 4 g, sheng ma 4 g, ku shen  12 g.

 

Add three and a half bowl of water. Boil down to

one bowl. Drink 2 hours after meals while warm.

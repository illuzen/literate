jue leng 厥冷,  (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

 

Jue leng of arms and legs is also called "arms and legs ni leng" and "four ni". Ni means going against. This syndrome refers to the coldness coming up from the extremes of the limbs going to elbows and knees.

liu yi san  六一散  (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 Translated and edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

 

Name of Formula: liu yi san (Created by Liu Wan Su of Yuan Dynasty)

Application: dark urine with difficulty in urinating. It is being used in urinary tract infection.

Composition: hua shi 6 portions, gan cao 1 portion.

Grind into fine powder.

Dosage: 9 g each time, take with cool drinking water.

Warning: if the urine is light in color and the volume is large, do not take this formula.

 

方名﹕六一散
應用:小便赤黃短澀。用於膀胱炎、尿道炎等屬濕熱者。
組成﹕滑石六份與 甘草 一份
研細末混合拌勻。
每服 9克，以涼開水調服。
注意﹕如小便清而長﹐無色而量多易排出﹐則不宜應用。

來源﹕劉完素（金元四大家之一,別號劉河間）所創

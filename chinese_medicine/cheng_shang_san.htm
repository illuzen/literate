cheng shang san 澄傷散  (外 用 )

 

Name of formula: cheng shang san

Application: for physical injuries, like broken bones, dislocated joints, injuries from impact. This formula is for external use only. For internal use (to take orally), remove  mu fu rong and take 10 g per day.

 

Composition:

gam2 zaa3 lak6 4 liang* ﹐san leng 3 liang ﹐ xu duan 2 liang,  dang gui wei (tail),   ru xiang  5 qian,  shang da huang  4 liang,  e zhu  3 liang, su mu 2 liang,

 chi shao 2 liang,  mo yao 5 qian,  pu huang 1 liang,   ping pian 5 qian,  jiang huang 1 liang,  mu fu rong 2 liang.

* If no gam2 zaa3 lak6, use jie gu mu as substistute.

Gam2 zaa3 lak6 is a Cantonese pronunciation for an herb imported from Vietnam.

Make into fine powder.

  by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu 朱興國 編

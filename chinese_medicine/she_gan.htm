she gan 射幹

 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu  朱興國譯

Modern Application:

現代應用：

Treating chyluria: she gan 15 g, boiled with water and add some sugar to taste. Make into 3 serving per day. Or make it into pills with water. Take 4 g each time, 3 times per day. 10 days is one therapy period. For chronic sickness, add chuan xiong 9 g, chi shao 12 g. For blood in the urine add sheng di, xian he cao 15 g each. Result: Treated 104 patients. Healed: 94 patients. Not effective: 10 patients. Among the healed, 9 had relapses within 1 to 6 months. After continuing with the above formula for one therapy period, all healed and no relapses.

治療乳糜尿：射幹15 克，水煎，加白糖適量。每天分三次服。或制成水丸。每次服4克。每 天三次。10天為一療程。病程長者加川芎9克，赤芍12克；血尿者加生地，仙鶴草各15克。共治療乳糜尿104例，痊愈94例，無效10例。痊愈 者之中有9例1～6月復發。繼續服上藥一療程後未再復發。[1]

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

Phytoestrogens Derived from Belamcanda Chinensis Have an Antiproliferative Effect on Prostate Cancer Cells in Vitro

在體外實驗，射干的根莖中提取的鳶尾黃素可抑制前列腺癌細胞的生長。在動物實驗 中，鳶尾黃素也能起到減緩前列腺癌細胞擴散的作用。
 

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

this herb may cause diarrhea. Not to use during pregnancy or no infection with heat, or in cases of pixu (spleen deficient) with diarrhea.

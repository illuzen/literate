di fu zi tang   地膚子湯

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu         按此看中文

Name of Formula: di fu zi tang (Source: Qian Jin Yao Fang)

Application: for for infection of the urinary tract: difficulty in urination, gonorrhea.

Composition:

di fu zi  15 g, zhi mu 15 g, huang qin 15 g, zhu ling 15 g, qu mai 10 g,

dong kui zi 10 g, zhi shi 10 g, sheng ma  9 g, tong cao  9 g, hai zao 12 g.

Make into decoction and use as tea.

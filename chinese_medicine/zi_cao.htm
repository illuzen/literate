zi cao 紫草  (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

 by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

radix arnebiae

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Arnebia euchroma (Royle) Johnst.(新 疆紫草,)

Lithospermum erythrorhizon Sieb. et Zucc., (紫草)

Arnebia guttata Bunge. (內蒙紫草)

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

zi2 cou2

Other Names:

別名﹕

miao 藐﹐cí cao 茈草，zi dan 紫丹﹐zi fu 紫芙，di xue 地 血，zi cao rong 紫草茸，ya xian cao 鴉銜草，zi cao gen 紫草根，shan zi cao 山紫草，hong shi gen 紅石根，zi gen cao 紫根草。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

gromwell root

Distribution:

產地﹕

 

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

sweet, salty, cold.

甘﹐鹹﹐寒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

heart, liver.

心﹐肝經。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

  1. anti-inflammation effect
  2. antipyretic, pain relieving, sedative
  3. antibiotic
  4. anti-tumor
  5. anti-pregnancy
  6. cardio-vascular effect
  7. effect on smooth muscles

1﹐抗炎作用
2﹐解熱﹐鎮痛﹐鎮靜作用。
3﹐抗病原微生物作用
4﹐抗腫瘤作用。
5﹐抗生育作用。
6﹐對心血管系統作用
7﹐對平滑肌作用

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

being used in preventing measles, jaundice, purpura, vomiting blood, nose bleeding, bleeding in the urinary tact, diarrhea with blood, boils, erysipelas, eczema, burns, constipation due to heat syndrome.

用於預防麻疹﹐熱病斑疹﹐黄疸﹐紫癜﹐吐衄尿血﹐血淋﹐血痢﹐痈腫瘡毒﹐丹毒﹐濕疹﹐燒傷﹐熱結便秘。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

乙酰紫草素困 (acetylshikonin),

紫草烷(alkannan), ,   

當歸酸紫草素 (alkannin angelate),

去氧紫草素（deoxyshikonin）

乙酰氧基異戊酰阿卡 寧 β-acetoxyisovalerylalkannin,

β-羥基異戊酰阿卡寧 β-hydroxyisovalerylalkannin.

(α-methyl-n-butyrylshikonin, Lithospermidin A﹐Lithospermidin B.

紫草寧（shikonin）
β-羟基異戊酰紫草素 beta, beta- dimethylacryloylshikonin, (β-Hydroxyosovalerylshkonin)
2，3－二甲基丙烯酰紫草素 ( Teracrylshikonin)
乙酰紫草素 (Acetyshikonin)
β,β′-二甲基丙烯酰紫草素 (β，β′-Dimethylacrylshikonin)
異丁酰紫草素 (Isobutyrylshikonin)
α-甲基－正丁酰紫草素 (α-Methyh-n-butyls-hikonin)
異戊酰紫草素 (Isobutvalerylshikonin)
去氢阿刊寧(Dehydroalkanon)
去氧紫草素 (Deoxyshikoonin)


紫草素及其衍生物含量:新疆紫草平均為 3.1%，紫草為1.05%， 新疆紫草大約為硬紫草的3倍

Dosage:

用量﹕

For taking orally: 4 to 10 g in decoction.

External use: Use appropriate amount, made into ointment with vegetable oil or make into gel by boiling.

內服，煎湯：4～10克。

外用適量，熬膏或用植物油浸泡塗擦。

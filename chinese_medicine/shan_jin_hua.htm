shan jin hua    山金花

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Flos Arnicae

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Arnica Montana L.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 arunika

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

 saan1 gam1 faa1

Other Names:

別名﹕

 shan jin che hua 山金車花

Common Name:

英文名﹕

leopard's bane, wolf's bane，mountain tobacco ， mountain arnica

Distribution:

產地﹕

 Originally produced in northern and middle part of Europe.

產歐洲北部和中部。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 acrid, sweet.

辛，甘。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

1. Analgesic,

2. Helenalin, an active ingredient of Arnica, is an anti-inflammatory sesquiterpene lactone from Arnica, selectively inhibits transcription factor NF-kappaB.

3. Anti-oxidant

4. Anti tumor

5. Choleretic

6. Uterine stimulant

  1. 止痛作用
  2. 消炎作用
  3. 抗氧
  4. 抗瘤
  5. 利膽
  6. 刺激子宮

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

external use only: sprains and bruises, muscle pain, arthritis pain and for stimulating the immune system.

外用：治療淤血，扭傷，肌肉痛、風濕痛及刺激免疫系統。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

helenalin

毒素與禁忌﹕

 do not use during breast feeding or during pregnancy.

授乳期間或懷孕期間忌用。

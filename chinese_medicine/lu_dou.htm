mung bean 綠荳  (in English and Chinese 中英文）

 

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Semen Phaseoli radiati

Biological name:

拉丁文學名﹕

  1. Vigna radiata (L.) R.Wilczek.

  2. Phaseolus aureus Roxb.  

  3. Phaseolus radiatus

  4. Phaseiolus mungo

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

ryokuzu

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

noktu

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

luk6 dau2

Other Names:

別名﹕

 

Common Name:

外文名﹕

Mung bean, Black dhal, Black gram, Black mung, Golden gram, Gram bean, Green gram, Red mung bean, Urd

Distribution:

產地﹕

Main production areas: India, Thailand, Afganistan, Phillipines, China, South East Asia and America.

 

世界上綠豆主產國有印度、泰國、阿富汗、菲律賓 、美國等國。
在中國黃河、淮河流域以及華北和東北平原各省都有綠豆栽培

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

sweet, cold, non toxic

 

甘寒無毒

Medical functions:

 

Channels (meridians) entered:

歸經﹕

heart, spleen

入心﹐胃經

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

rids of edema, possesses antipyretic effect, diuretic effects, rids of headache, calming, antidote for toxicity of arsenic, and poisons from plants or minerals.

 

 

消腫下氣、壓熱解毒、治丹毒、煩熱風疹、利尿止渴、厚腸胃。作枕明目、治頭風、頭痛、補 益元氣、和調五臟、安精神、行十二經脈、潤皮膚、解一切草木金石砒霜毒

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

See appendix below. 看附錄。

Dosage:

用量﹕

30 ~ 60 g

Samples of formulae

:處方舉例﹕

Recipe 1: mung bean soup

mung bean soup: lu do 60 g, chen pi 3 g. Simmer with appropriate amount of water until mung bean is soft and tender to make soup.

 

Recipe 2: chou cao mung bean soup

For cooling and relieving swelling; as food supplement for those with nose and throat cancer, headache, blood in mucus from the nose, and uncomfortable dry throat.

Composition: chou cao 20 g (put in last with rock sugar), rock sugar to taste, sheng di huang 40 g, lu dou 120 g, chen pi 1 piece (to taste). Add appropriate amount of water and simmer till lu dou (mung bean) is tender; then add chou cao and rock sugar. Continue to simmer until the rock sugar is completely dissolved.

Editor note: Dot not use this formula in cases of  pixu (spleen deficient) weak digestion, chronic pain in the lower back with shenxu (kidney deficient), especially women with this type of body function.

臭草綠荳湯

效用：清熱解毒，養胃生津，消腫散結。
凡患上了鼻咽癌病症，頭痛，鼻涕带血，咽喉乾燥不適，可用此湯作食療。
原料：臭 草２０克 (後下)，生 地黃４ ０克，绿豆１２０克， 陳皮1片 ﹐冰糖適量 (後下)。 加水適量﹐ 以慢火煎至綠荳熟爛。然後加入臭草﹐冰糖。繼續煎至冰糖溶化。

來源﹕中華素食網
　　
編者按：脾 虛，腎 虛腰痛及身體虚寒者忌用此方。有此類體質的婦 女尤忌。

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

 

Cautions:

禁忌﹕

Dot not use in cases of  pixu (spleen deficient) weak digestion, chronic pain in the lower back with shenxu (kidney deficient), especially women with this type of body function.

脾虛，腎 虛腰痛及身體虚寒者忌食。有此類體質的婦女尤 忌。

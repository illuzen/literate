xià guān S7;ST7下關   (in English and Chinese 中英文)
 

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu 朱興國編譯

 

Location: with the mouth closed, about the width of the middle finger in front of the tragus, in a depression beneath zygomatic arch ( it protrudes when the mouth is opened).

 

Indications:  deafness, tinnitus, middle ear infection, periodontitis, toothache, lockjaw, droopy eyes and mouth, and trigeminal neuralgia..
.

 

下關穴 S7;ST7

屬足陽明胃經的面部經穴。
定位：在臉部耳屏前方一中指寬，當顴弓與下頜切跡所形成的凹陷中（張口時隆起）。
主治: 耳聾，耳鳴，聤耳，牙 周炎，牙痛，口噤，口眼歪斜，臉 部神經麻痺。

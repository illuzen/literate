Snoring of Old People 老年打鼾

Translated and Edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu     (in English and Chinese 中英文）

Name of Formula: niu bang fang

Application: snoring of old people

Composition:

niu bang zi, zhu ma gen, gan cao.

Boil with water and use as tea.

Effectiveness:

  1. very effective: 89%

  2. effective: 15%

  3. not effective: 5%

Source: Journal of Sichuan Traditional Chinese Medicine , Issue 11, 2002.

Also see: San Zhong Kui Jian Tang

老年打鼾

處方﹕牛蒡子，苧麻根，甘 草.。

顯效80％, 有效15％, 無效5％.

出處﹕ 四川中醫 2002年 11期。

請參考﹕散腫潰堅湯

A Trade Formula

Trade Name of Herb: Hong Cao Snore Stopping Pills

Composition: hong jing tian, ma huang, ban bian lian,  niu bang zi, mu xiang, gan cao.
Make into pills

 

成藥：

紅草止鼾顆粒

藥方組成：紅景天，麻 黃，半邊蓮，牛蒡子，土木 香 ，甘 草。

wu zhu yu tang <Shang Han Lun>

　

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

 

Syndromes: Unpleasant sensation in the solar plexus area, headache, nausea, vomiting. Vasomotor dysfunction type of headache (like migraine), muscle tension type of headache.

　

Prescription:

　

wu zhu yu 6 g, ren shen 6 g, da zao 4 pieces, sheng jiang 18 g.

　

Boil with water and use as tea.

　

 

Please set your browser to read traditional Chinese BIG5.

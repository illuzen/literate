gan sui 甘遂

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Radix Euphorbiae Kansui

Botanical name:

拉丁生物學名﹕

Euphorbia sieboldiana Morr. et Deene.；Euphorbia kansui T.N. Liou ex T.P. Wang

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

kansui

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

kamsu

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

gam1 seoi6

Other Names:

別名﹕

mao er yan (being called in Northwestern part of China), hua gu dan (melting bone pills), gan ze, zhong shou hua gen(roots of hand swelling flowers), xuan gen zi

貓兒眼西北，化骨丹，甘澤，腫手花根，萱根子

Common Name:

英文名﹕

kansui root

Distribution:

產地﹕

 mainly distributed in Shaanxi, Shanxi, and Henan provinces.

主產於陜西，山西，河南等地。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

bitter, sweet, cold, toxic.

苦，甘，寒，有毒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

kidney, large intestine, lung.

腎，大腸，肺。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

Abortive effect

laxative effect

immune system inhibiting effect on rabbit

anti leukemia effect

effect on heart muscle

toxic effect

堕胎作用

瀉下作用

抑制家兔免疫系统作用

抗白血病作用

對心肌作用

毒性作用

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

瀉水逐飲。用於水腫脹滿，胸腹積水，痰飲積聚，氣逆喘咳，二便不利，消腫散結：外用可治癰腫瘡瘍。

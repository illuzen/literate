bo ye 柏葉

 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

Cacumen Biotae Orientalis

Botanical name:

學名﹕

  1. Biota orientalis (L.) Engl.

  2. Platycladus stricta (Spach.),

  3. Platycladus orientalis ((L.)Franco.)

Distribution:

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性 味﹕

bitter, tart and slightly cold.

Channels (meridians) entered:

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

Stops bleeding, harmonizing blood, inhibits cough, anti-bacteria, anti-inflammation.

The seed is laxative, capable of easing pain and sedative. It is used in decoction in palpitations, insomnia, nervous disorders and constipation in the seniors.
 

止血 ，和血 ，鎮咳，抗菌，消炎。

柏子仁用于止痛﹐鎮靜﹐緩瀉劑。 煎湯治心陣跳﹐失眠﹐神經緊張﹐與老人便秘﹐

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Actions & Indications:

  1. bleeding: vomiting blood, nose bleeding, diarrhea with blood, urine with blood, vagina bleeding not during menses

  2. chronic bronchitis

  3. heat type of cough, dry cough

  4. bark and roots being used externally for burn or scalding

  5. for alopecia, use tincture made from fresh ce bo ye.

主治﹕

  1. 各種出血﹕吐血，衄 血，血 痢，便血，尿血，崩漏﹔

  2. 慢性氣管炎，

  3. 熱咳，燥咳﹐

  4. 外用燙火傷。

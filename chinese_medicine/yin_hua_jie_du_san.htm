yin hua jie du san 銀花解毒散

Translated and edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

 

Name of Formula: yin hua jie du san

Application: periodontitis (pyorrhea), gum infection

jin yin hua 9 g, zhu ye 9 g, xuan shen 9 g, lian qiao 9 g, sheng gan cao 4.5 g, boil with water to make tea.

Adjustment:

  1.  bleeding, add sheng di, mao gen;

  2.  forming of puss, add zao jiao, fried chuan shan jia.

xu duan 續斷       (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Present Day Appication:

現 代 應 用 ﹕
  1. miscarriage with bleeding.
  2. treatment of soft tissue injuries

 

  1. 治療先兆性 流產。
  2. 治療軟組織損傷。

Pharmaceutical Name:

英文藥名﹕

 Dipsaci Radix

Botanical name:

學名﹕

 Dipsacus asperoides, C. Y.Cheng et TM Ai

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 zokudan

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 sokdan

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

 zuk6  dyun6

Other Names:

別名﹕

 liu han 六汗, chuan duan 川斷。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

 dipsacus

Distribution:

產地﹕

 middle and southern parts of China.

分 佈 華中﹐華南等地。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 bitter, acrid, slightly warm.

味苦、辛，性微溫。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

 liver, kidneys.

入肝、腎二經。
Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 
  1. inhibits and reverse osteoporosis,
  2. promotes healing of injured bones
  3. alleviates the contraction of the uterus, thus calming the fetus
  4. improves immune system
  5. inhibits diplococcus pneumoniae
  6. protects from lacking of vitamin E
  7. kills Trichomoniasis
  8. helps uterus to grow and develop: helped the uteri of mice to continue to grow and devlop after removal of their ovaries.

 

  1. 抗骨 質 疏鬆作用:  川續斷 水煎液對去卵巢大鼠之骨 質疏鬆,可以使之逆轉 。
  2. 促進骨損 傷 愈合 
  3. 對子宫的作用﹕對在體 子宫的作用：，降低其收缩幅度和張力；安胎作用。
  4. 对免疫功能的影响  川续断水煎液能提高小鼠缺氧能力，延長小鼠负重游泳持 續 時間，促進小鼠巨噬细胞的吞噬功能。
  5. 對肺炎雙球菌有抑制作用
  6. 抗维生素E缺乏症，
  7. 殺滅陰道毛滴虫（ 濃 度 為 0.4mg/ml）的作用﹐
  8. 促進子宮發育﹕促進去卵巢小鼠子宮生長發育。

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

 For: chronic pain in loin and knees due to shenxu (kidney deficient), physical injuries, heavy bleeding during menses, pain during menses, heavy vaginal bleeding (not during menses), abdomen pain, vaginal discharge, hemorrhoids.

 腎虛腰 膝痠痛﹐ 跌打損傷，四肢腫痛﹐月經過多﹐月經痛﹐崩漏﹐腹痛﹐帶 下﹐遺精﹐ 癰痔腫毒。

diu liao bang 丟了棒   (白桐樹)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

A manuscript... writing and translation in progress

Pharmaceutical name:

Radix Claoxylon indici

Folium Claoxylon indici,

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Claoxylon indicum, Claoxylon polot

Securidaca inappendiculata Hassk (蝉翼藤) (being used in area of Yu Lin of Guangxi provinces as this herb)

Pron. in Japanese:

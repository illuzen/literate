da huang mu dan pi tang  

Translated and edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Name of Formula: da huang mo dan pi tang   《source: Jin Gui Yao Lue  》

Application: acute appendicitis, infection of the organs in the pelvis area, salpingitis after salpingorrhaphy.

Prescription:

da huang 10 g, dong gua ren 6 g, mu dan pi 2.5 g, mang xiao 7 g,  tao ren 2.5 g.

.Boil with water and use as tea.

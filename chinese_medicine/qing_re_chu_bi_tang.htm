Qing Re Chu Bi Tang (Rid of heat and arthritis decoction)  (in Enlgish and Chinese 中英文)

 清熱除痺湯

Translated and edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Name of Formula: Qing Re Chu Bi Tang (Rid of heat and arthritis decoction)

Application: Pain in the body, red, hot and swelling in joints, after giving birth

Composition: ren dong teng 30 g, wei ling xian 9 g, qing feng teng 15 g, hai feng teng 15 g, luo shi teng 15 g, fang ji 9 g, sang zhi 30 g, zhui di feng 9 g.

Boil with water and use as tea.
 

清熱除痺湯

方名: 清熱除痺湯 （劉奉伍婦科經驗）
功效: 清熱散濕，疏風活血。
主治: 產後身疼，關節紅腫灼痛。

組成: 忍冬藤1兩，威靈仙 3錢，青風藤 5錢，海風藤  5錢，

絡石藤 5錢，防 己 3錢，桑 枝1兩，追地風 3錢。
水煎服。

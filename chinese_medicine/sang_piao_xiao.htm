sang piao xiao 桑螵蛸   (in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 Ootheca Mantidis

Zoological  name:

動物學名﹕

Tenodera sinensis Saussure （大 刀螂）

Statilia maculata Thunberg（小 刀螂）

Hierodula patellifera Serville （巨 斧螳螂）

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 sohyosho

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 sangpyoso

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

 song1 piu1 siu1

Other Names:

別名﹕

 tuan piao xiao 團螵蛸，chang piao xiao 長螵蛸，hei piao xiao黑螵蛸

Common Name:

英文名﹕

 mantis egg casing

Distribution:

產地﹕

 Guangxi, Yunnan, Hebei, Gansu, Liaoning (Hierodula patellifera ), Zhejiang, Jiangsu, Anhui, Shandong, Hubei (Statilia maculata) , Hebei, Shaanxi (Hierodula patellifera ).

廣西，云南，河北，甘肅，遼寧（巨 斧螳螂），浙江，江蘇，安徽，山東，湖北（小 刀螂)，陕西（巨 斧螳螂）。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 sweet, salty, neutral.

甘，鹹，平。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

 liver, kidney

歸肝，腎經。
Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

  

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

 Being used in spermatorrhea, incontinence,frequent urination, cloudy urine.

用於遺精滑精，遺尿尿頻，小便白濁。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

  

Dosage:

用量﹕

 Taking orally: 5 to 10 g in decoction, or3 to 5 g  in powder form, or in pill form.

External use: appropriate amount in powder form or make into paste with oil and apply to affected area.

內服：煎湯，5-10g；研末，3-5g；或入丸劑。外用：適量，研末撒或油調敷。

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

 san piao xiao san 桑螵蛸散

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

  

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

 Use care in cases yinxu (yin deficient) with heat.

陰虛火 旺或膀胱有熱者慎服。

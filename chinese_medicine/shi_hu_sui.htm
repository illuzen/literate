shi hu sui  石胡荽  

 

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

A manuscript... writing and translation in progress

Present Day Applications:

現代應用﹕

  

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 Centipedae Herba

Botanical name:

學名﹕

 Centipeda minima (Linn.) A.Br.et Aschers 

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

  

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

  

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

  

Other Names:

別名﹕

 tian hu sui 天胡荽，ye yuan sui 野圆荽，e bu shi cao 鹅不食草，jichang cao 雞腸草。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

  

Distribution:

產地﹕

 Mainly produced in Zhejiang, Jiangsu, Hubei, Guangdong. Also produced in Anhui, Jiangxi, Fujian, Henan, Guangxi and other provinces.

主產於浙江，江蘇，湖北，廣東。安徽，江西，福建，河南，廣西等地亦產。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 acrid, warm.

辛，溫。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

 lung, liver.

肺，肝。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

  

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

 For cold or flu with headache, cough with abundance of phlegm, suffy nose, chronic sinus infammation, whooping cough, chronic bronchitis, conjunctivitis, malaria.

用於風寒頭痛，咳嗽痰多，鼻塞不通，鼻淵流涕，百日咳，慢性支氣管炎，結膜炎，瘧疾。

Campesterol 菜油甾醇
Stigmasterol 豆甾醇
β-sitosterol β-谷甾醇

Tannin 鞣質
Resin 樹脂
Coumarin 香豆素
Lactone 內酯

Dosage:

用量﹕

 For decoction: 6 to 9 g. External application: appropriate amount.

内服：6～9 克。外用：適量。

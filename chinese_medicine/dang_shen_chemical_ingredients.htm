Chemical ingredients of dang shen 黨參的化學成份

 

Translated and edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu  (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

Sterols:甾醇類有：
α-Spinasterolα-菠菜甾醇
Stigmasterol豆甾醇
α-Spinasterol-β-D-glucosideα-菠菜甾醇-β-D-葡萄糖甙
Stigmasterol-β-D-glucoside豆甾醇-β-D-葡萄糖甙
α-Spinasteroneα-菠菜甾酮
Stigmasta-7, 22-dien-3-one豆甾酮
Sugar and glycosides:糖和甙類有：
Inulin菊糖
Fructose果糖
Four types of polysaccharide that contain fructose四種含果糖的雜多糖
Syringin丁香甙
n-Hexyl-β-D-glucopyranosideβ-D-吡喃葡萄糖己醇甙
Ethyl-α-D-fructofuranosideα-D-呋喃果糖乙醇甙
Alkaloids and those contain nitrogen生物鹼及含氮成份有：
Choline膽鹼
n-Butyl-allophanate正丁基脲基甲酸酯
Codopiloic acid黨參酸
5- Hydroxy-2-pyridinemethanol5-羥基-2-羥甲基吡啶
Nicotine煙鹼
Evaporating oils:揮發性成份有：
Caproic acid己酸
Enanthic acid庚酸
Caprylic acid辛酸
Pelargonic acid壬酸
Lauric acid十二酸
Azelaic acid壬二酸
Myristic acid十四酸
Pentadecanoic acid十五酸
Palmitic acid十六酸
Margaric acid十八酸
Octadecadienoic acid十八碳二烯酸
2,4-Nonadienic acid2,4-壬二烯酸
n-Pentadecane正十五烷
α-Curcumeneα-姜黃烯
n-Heptadecane正十七烷
n-Octadecane正十八烷
n-Nonadecane正十九烷
n-Heneicosane正廿一烷
n-Docosane正廿二烷
Methyl myristate十四酸甲酯
Methyl pentadecanoate十五酸甲酯
Methyl palmitate十六酸甲酯
Methyl stearate硬脂酸甲酯
Methyl octadecadienoate十八碳二烯酸甲酯
Pinene蒎烯
Ethyl palmitate十六酸乙酯
Methyl caprylate辛酸甲酯
Also contains triterpenes and other:尚含三萜類及其他成分：
Taraxerol蒲公英萜醇
Taraxeryl acetate乙酰蒲公英萜醇
Friedelin木栓酮
AtractylnolideⅡ、Ⅲ蒼術內酯Ⅱ、Ⅲ
Syringaldehyde丁香醛
Vanillic acid香莢蘭酸
2-Furancarboxylate2-呋喃羥酸酯
Codonolactone黨參內酯
5-Hydroxy-methyl furaldehyde5-羥甲基糠醛
5-Methoxyl-furaldehyde5-甲氧基糠醛

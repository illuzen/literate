lu e mei   綠萼梅  （in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Flos Armeniacae mume

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Armeniaca mume Sieb.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

nok ngak mae

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

luk6 ngok6 mui4

Other Names:

別名﹕

bai hua mei

Common Name:

英文名﹕

Japanese apricot flower with green calyx

Distribution:

產地﹕

Originated in China. Produced in Sichuan, Zhejiang, Fujian,  Guangdong, Hunan, Guizhou,Jiangxi provinces.  Those produced in Changxin of Zhejiang are deemed  best.

原産中國。主産於四川，福建，廣東，湖南，貴州，江西等地。産於浙江長興者被認為最佳。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

Sour, astringent, neutral.

味甘，微辛，性微寒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

 liver and  lung

肝，肺。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Rrelieving stomachache caused by dominant liver qi; globus hystericus (sensation of obstruction of throat during hysteria attacks)  anorexia, dizziness, scrofula (TB of lymph nodes), resolving phlegm.

用於：肝氣盛所引起的腹痛，梅核氣，厭食， 眩晕，淋巴結核，化痰。
 

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

Contain evaporationg oil, mainly benzaldehyde,(phenylaldehyde, benzene carbona C7H6O), isoeugenol, benzoic acid.

含揮發油，主要有苯甲醛 (安息香醛)，異丁香油酚，苯甲酸 (安息香酸)。

Daly Dosage:

每日用量﹕

3  ~ 6 g

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

 

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

 

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

Long term usage are not suitable for those who are yinxu (yin deficient) with serious sickness, accompanied with red tongue without fur, dry mouth, and preferring cold drink.

陰虛重症見舌紅 無苔 ，少津，口乾喜冷飲等仍不宜長期服用。

The variety that has green calyx is called lu e mei, literally means apricot (flowers) with green calyx. In the classics Ben Cao Gang Mu it is called bai hua mei, literally mean white flower plum.

烏梅，梅花（梅）
原文：Prunus mume（Sieb.）Sieb. et Zucc.（61、268页）
補正：Armeniaca mume Sieb.
注解：本種為杏屬（Armeniaca）植物，而《藥典》所採用的本種屬名（Prunus），現為李屬的屬名。《藥典》所載其學名為異名。　(沈 保安﹐安徽省芜湖市药品检验所 241001)

 

   其花 萼绿色的叫綠萼梅。本 草綱目稱之為白花梅。

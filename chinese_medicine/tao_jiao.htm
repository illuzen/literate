tao jiao 桃膠        (in Engish and Chinese 中英文)
by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Resina Persicae

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Amygdalus persica L .[Prunus persica (L.) Batsch.]

Amygdalus davidiana (Carr.)C.de Vos ex Henry [Persica davidiana Carr.;Prunus davidiana (Carr.) Franch.]

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

tokyo

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

dogyo

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

tou4 gaau1

Other Names:

別名﹕

tao hua lei (tears of peach flower) 桃花淚

Common Name:

英文名﹕

peach resin

Distribution:

產地﹕

Hebei, Shanxi, Shaanxi, Gansu, Shandong, Henan, Sichuan, Yunnan provinces of China.

河北，山西，陝西，甘肅，山東，河南，四川，雲南等地。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

Sweet, bitter, neutral.

甘，苦，平。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

large intestine, bladder.

大腸，膀胱。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Being used in calculus in urinary tract, blood in urine, dysentery, chyluria.

用於治：石淋，血淋，痢疾，乳糜尿。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

 

galactose

半乳糖

rhamnose

鼠李糖

a-glucuronic acid

α-葡萄糖醛酸

 

 

Daly Dosage:

每日用量﹕

 

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

For treatment of pain caused by stone in urinary tract:

Use tao jiao the size of dai zao and mix with 300 cc of drinking water. Take 3 times a day. (Use cool drinking water in summer and warm drinking water in winter.)[1]

For treatment of diabetes: wash tao jiao with luke warm water. Cook in a small pot. Add any flavoring if desire but do not add sugar. Take orally 30 to 60 grams each time. [2]

治石淋作痛：桃木膠如棗大，夏以冷水三合，冬以湯三合和服，日三服，當下石，石盡即止。(《古今錄驗方》

治糖尿病：桃膠，用微溫水洗凈，放在小鍋內煮食，隨便加些調味鹽類亦可(但不要加入甜味)。每次服一至二兩。(《草藥驗方交流集》)

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

 

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

[1] "Effective formulae from Ancient Time to Today" 《古今錄驗方》

[2] Effective Herbal Formule Comunication Set 《草藥驗方交流集》

Note: gum arabic are being sold as tao jiao or tao an zhu (beads shape) or tao jiao powder (in powder form) which is incorrect.

Gum arabic is not a pure chemical compound. It does not have chemical formula.

The secretion of trunks of peach trees, plum  trees, apricot  trees, cherry  trees are call tao jiao (peach resin).

 

註：阿拉伯樹膠簡稱阿拉伯膠，商業上誤稱做桃膠，桃胺珠(粒狀)或桃膠粉(粉狀)。 由於它不是一個純粹的化合物，故無分子式。

桃，李，杏，櫻桃等樹幹分泌的膠稱為桃膠。

 

血淋：小便短澀.滴瀝刺痛，欲出未盡，小腹拘急，或痛引腰腹.小便紅赤，或夾有血塊，甚者可溺出純血，臨床上以尿血而兼小便滴瀝澀痛者，稱為血淋。

慢性腎盂腎炎，尿道綜合徵，膀胱癌等病症表現有膀胱刺激症狀並伴有血尿者均屬"血淋" 範疇

DMBA       (in English and Chinese 中 英文)
 

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

 

DMBA is an auto-immune suppressor and a powerful organ-specific laboratory carcinogen. It is also known as 7,12-dimethylbenz[α]anthracene. This substance is widely used in many research laboratories for studying cancer. DMBA serves as a tumor initiator by making necessary mutations.

DMBA 是自體免疫抑制剤，是一個強大的器官性實驗室致癌物。它也被稱為7,12 - 二甲苯并[α]蒽。這種物質被廣泛應用在許多研究實驗室作為研究癌症 用。DMBA 是用來做細胞突變，腫瘤的引發劑。

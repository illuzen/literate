Herbs that are Not Compatible with Certain Drugs

 by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Names of Drugs

Names of Herbs

Reasons:

ampicillin ( Omnipen, Polycillin, Principen)

jia zhu tao夾竹桃
wan nian qing萬年青
fu shou cao福壽草
ampicillin is a strong inhibitor for Psuedomonas Aureginosa. If it use with the cardiac stimulating herb of jia zhu tao, wan nian qiang or fu shou cao it can cause hypokalemia and create cardiac toxicity for the herbs.

Tetracyclines

shi gao石膏
han shui shi寒水石
niu huang jie du pian牛黃解毒 片

herbs contain calcium sulfate which may combine with tetracyclines and render it ineffective.

Iron compounds, vitamin B1

shi liu pi石榴皮
wu bei zi五倍子
da huang大 黃
di yu地 榆  
hu zhang虎 杖

the herbs contain large amount of tannin acid and may result in tannin salt

Aluminium Hydroxide , Aminophylline , Sodium Bicarbonate

 
bao he wan保和 丸
liu wei di huang wan六味地黃 丸

the drugs are alkaline and the herbs are acidic.

Aspirin

 
lu rong鹿 茸
gan cao甘 草

the herbs posses the effect steroid and aspirin can stimulate the mucus lining of the stomach

corticosteroids
ginseng人參
 possible additive effect

cyclosporine

guan ye lian qiao (St. John's wort)貫葉連翹

 

Digitalis type of drug

 
zhen zhu mu珍珠母
long gu龍 骨
shi gao石膏

the herbs contain calcium ions and can increase the toxicity of digitalis and can result in arrhythmia

Digitalis type of drug

 
gan cao甘 草
  

the herb can increase the function of the drug and may cause cardiac exhaustion

Diuretics for expelling potassium

 
gan cao 甘 草
  

the herb has mineralocorticoid effect which can retain sodium and expel potassium and may cause over expelling K and result in low overly potassium.

Erythromycin. (Erythromycinum, Erythrocin, Erythrogran, Robimycin 紅黴素)

chuan xin lian穿心蓮
  
Erythromycin can inhibit the function of chuan xin lian in promoting the ability of white cells in killing bacteria.

Sulphonamides

 
wu mei烏梅
shan zha山 楂
nu zhen zi女 貞 子
wu wei zi五味子

 

may cause increase of uric acid crystal formation

Sodim bicarbonate, Aminophylline , theophyline

wu mei烏梅
shan zha山 楂

wu wei zi五味子

lowering the effectiveness of the drugs

warfarin, heparin

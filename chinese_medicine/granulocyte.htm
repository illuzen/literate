Granulocyte

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Leucocytes  (white blood cells) with conspicuous cytoplasmic granules. The human granulocytes are classified as polymorphonuclear leucocytes which are subdivided according to the staining properties of the granules into eosinophils, basophils and neutrophils using a Romanovsky type stain.

da chai hu tang  大 柴胡湯

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu     (in English and Chinese 中英文)

Name of Formula: da chai hu tang   (Shang Han Lun)

Application: typhus, influenza, malaria, pneumonia. The sickness is changing  from Shaoyang disease to Yangming disease, with syndrome of fever or alternating with chills and fever, fullness in chest with pain, or nausea, vomiting, loss of appetite, dry tongue and yellow moss, constipation, abdominal pain and dislike being pressed, strong pulse. This formula can be used for above syndrome.

 

Composition:

shao_yao  12 g, chai hu  7.5 g, da huang 4 g, huang qin   12 g, sheng jiang  3 slices, zhi shi  12 g,hong zao  3 pieces, remove pits, ban xia    12 g.

Add 2.5 bowls of water. Simmer down to 80% of a bowl. Take 2 hours after meals, while it is still warm.

大柴胡湯 ( 傷寒論)

各種熱性傳染病，如傷寒、流行性感冒、猩紅熱、丹毒、瘧疾、肺炎等病的經過中，欲從少陽病移轉為陽明病，而發熱或寒熱往來、胸 膈苦滿，或噁心、嘔吐、食慾不振、舌乾而有黃苔，便秘 ，腹痛拒按，脈有力者，可用本方。

處方組成﹕

 芍藥 三 錢﹐  柴 胡 二 錢﹐ 大黃 一錢﹐ 黃 芩三 錢﹐ 生 姜 三片﹐

 枳 實 三 錢﹐紅 棗 去核 三枚﹐ 半 夏 三 錢。

水 兩 碗 半。 煎 八 分 。飯後二小時溫服

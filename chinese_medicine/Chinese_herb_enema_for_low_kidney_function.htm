Chinese herb enema for treatment of low kidney function

Translated and Explained by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu                 

(2). Method of treatment

A self composed formula: Xie Chu Pai Du Tang (Purging Impurity and Detoxification Decoction.

Composition: da huang 15g, pu gong ying 30g, dang gui12g,   dan shen20g, ze xie15g, dong gua pi 30g，dan pi10g, gan cao 6g

Make into thick decoction of 200 ml for enema of once a day, using lying down on right side position. After enema, rest in bed for 2 hours.

Control group: use Coated aldehyde oxystarch, 3 times per day, one bag of 5g each time.

The treatment period of both treatments was 30 days. Besides using this treatment, supporting treatments were also being used as symptoms arose.  Before treatment and during treatment period of 1~2 weeks, observation and recording of changes of symptoms, blood tests, urine normality, liver function, kidney function, electrolyte etc.

2 治療方法

治療組給予中藥自擬方瀉濁排毒湯（大黃  15g，蒲 公英30g，當 歸12g，  丹 參20g，澤 瀉15g，冬瓜皮30g，丹皮10g， 生甘 草6g）濃煎200ml灌腸，每日1次，取右側臥位，灌腸后靜臥2h。對照組給予包醛氧化澱粉，每天3次，每次1包（5g），兩組 療程均為30天，兩組除本療法外，均給予對症及支持治療。治療前和治療期間1～2周觀察記錄症狀及體征變化，查血，尿常規;肝，腎功能，電解質等。

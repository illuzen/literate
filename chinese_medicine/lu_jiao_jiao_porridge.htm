lu jiao jiao porridge 鹿角膠粥

 

Edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

 

Name of formula: lu jiao jiao porridge (deer horn glue porridge) (Ben Cao Gang Mu)

 

Application: for shenyangxu (kidney yang deficient) resulting impotence, premature ejaculation, spermatorrhea, chronic pain in the loin, cold and deficient uterus, unable to conceive, excessive bleeding not during menses, vagina discharge.

 

Ingredients: lu jiao jiao 15 to 20 g, rice 100 g, sheng jiang 3 slices.

Boil rice with water to make porridge. Add lu jiao jiao and sheng jiang (raw ginger) while porridge is still boiling.

 

Take 1 to 2 times a day. 3 to 5 days is one therapy period.

 

【方名】鹿角膠粥 《本草綱目》

【原料】鹿角膠15～20克 粳米100克 生薑 3片

【制作】先煮粳米作粥，待沸後，放入鹿角胶、生薑同煮为稀粥。

【用法】每日1～2次。3～5天為一療程。

【功效】補腎陽，益精血。適應於 腎陽虛 所致的陽萎、早泄、遗精、腰痛、婦人子宫虚冷、不孕、崩漏、帶下等。

【宜忌】陰虚火旺、口乾舌燥、尿黄便秘或感冒發熱者忌服。適宜于冬季服用。

ping xiao san 平消散

　

translated annotated by:

Joe Hing kwok Chu                按 此 看 中 文

The following formulae are for reference use only.

Ping xiao san (source : Study of Chinese Medicine for Tumor.)

For any type of leukemia when it first started and the symptoms are not serious. Use this formula and plus dan fan san formula.

Composition of the ping xiao san formula:

zhi ke 30 g, xiao shi 18 g, wu ling zhi 15 g, yu jin 18 g,

bai fan 18 g, xian he cao  18 g, gan qi (stir fried) 6 g, zhi ma qian zi  12 g.

Grind into fine powder. Put into capsules. Take 1 1/2 g to 6 g each time, 3 times per day, with boiled drinking water.

　

dan fan san

Composition:

chicken egg shell, baked to yellowish color, 60 g; liao jiang shi 60 g, zao fan 50 g, hong ren shen 30 g, shan ci gu 50 g.

　

Grind into fine powder and put into capsules. Take 1 to 3 g each time, 3 times per day, with boiled drinking water.

 平消散  《中醫癌瘤 学》

各種類型的白血病﹐病初起﹐而症狀

輕者可用此方加蛋礬散共同 内服。

枳 殼  30克﹐ 硝石 18克﹐ 五靈脂 15克﹐ 鬱金18克﹐

白礬18克﹐ 仙 鶴 草 18克,  乾漆（炒)  6克﹐ 制 馬前子﹐(番木鱉) 12克。

制法及服法：共研細末，裝入膠囊。每次服1.5—6克，日服3次。開水送服。

蛋礬散 《中醫癌瘤学》

組成﹕

雞蛋殼(焙黄) 60克﹐料薑石 60克﹐ 皂 礬 50克﹐ 紅參  30克﹐山慈姑 50克。

制法及服法：共研為 細粉，裝入膠囊。每服1—3克，日服3次。開水送服。

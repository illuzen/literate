Essential oils of rhizomes of galanga (nan jiang)

南薑之揮發油    按此看中文。

Essential oils from both fresh and dried rhizomes of galangal have antimicrobial 
activities against bacteria, fungi, and parasite. It has been reported and  that terpinen-4-ol, 
one of the monoterpenes in the essential oil from fresh galangal rhizomes, contains an 
antimicrobial activity against trichophyton mentagrophytes (a fungus that causes infection 
of hair, skin and nails).
Acetoxychavicol acetate, a compound isolated from an ether-soluble extract of dried 
rhizomes, is active against some bacteria and many dermatophyte species. It is 
anti-ulcer and anti-tumor agents as well as an inhibitor of chemically induced 
carcinogenesis.
(A. Murakami, K. Toyota, S. Ohura, K. Koshimizu and H. Ohigashi)

南薑之揮發油

Translated by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu 朱興國譯

乾或鮮南薑之揮發油抗細菌，霉菌，與寄生虫之作用。鮮南薑的單帖烯中之 鬆油烯-4-醇能抗 鬚髮癬菌 Trichophyton mentagrophytes。（導致頭髮，皮膚，指甲，或趾甲之霉菌 ）。
1′-乙酰氧基胡椒 酚乙酸酯是由乾南薑用乙醚提出的成份之一 ，有抵抗幾種細菌與多種導致皮膚病的霉菌，亦有抗癌，抗潰瘍與能抑制化學品引起的致癌作用 。
葡萄球菌之導致食物中毒：最初在1894年有研究發現葡萄球菌型腸胃炎是由吃進葡萄球菌產生的腸毒素所致。
葡萄球菌是一種非孢子格蘭陽性之球菌。有些種類能產生腸毒素。
 

註：金黃色釀膿葡萄 球菌(Staphylococcus aureus) [或簡稱葡萄球菌(staph)]是 人們皮膚. 上和鼻子裡的細菌。通常葡萄球菌是無害的,但有時 可以導致感染和嚴重的疾病。

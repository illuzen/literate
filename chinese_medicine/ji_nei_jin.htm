ji nei jin 雞內金

 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

 

Click  here to see picture.        按此看圖。

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Endothelium Corneum Gigeriae Galli.

Zoological name:

學名﹕

Gallus gallus domesticus Brisson

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

keinaikin

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

myongnaegkim

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

gai1 noi6 gam1

Other Names:

別名﹕

ji zhun pi, nei jin, jiu ji jin, hua shi dan, ji zhen pi, ji jin.

雞肫皮，內金 、炙雞金，化石膽，雞胗皮，雞筋。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

Chicken Gizzard's Stomach Lining

Distribution:

產地﹕

throughout China

全國都有。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

sweet, neutral.

甘，平。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

spleen, stomach, small intestine, bladder.

脾﹐胃﹐小腸 ﹐膀胱。
Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

increases digestive juices thus shortens the emptying time of the bowels

增加消化液﹐ 縮短排便時間。

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Being used in weak digestion, vomiting, diarrhea, children worm parasites , bed wetting, spermatorrhea. amenorrhea.

用于食積不消 ，嘔吐瀉痢，小儿疳積，尿路結 石 ﹐肝胆結石﹐遺尿﹐遺精﹐閉經[1]。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

ventriculin, keratin, bilatriene, vitamin B1, B2

胃激素, 角 蛋白, 膽汁三烯, 維生素B1﹐維生素B2。

Dosage:

用量﹕

For decoction: 3 to 10 g.

In Powder Form: 1.5 to 3 g.  The effect is deemed to be better than that of decoction.

煎劑﹕  3－10 g。

研末服﹕每次1.5－3g，效果可能比入煎 劑 好。

Samples of formulae:

處方舉 例﹕

stones in the urinary tract:jin qian cao,, ji nei jin

bed wetting of children and adult frequent urination : sang piao xiao, long gu, mo li

尿石﹕金 錢草﹐雞 內金

小兒尿失禁﹐ 成人尿頻﹕桑螵蛸﹐ 龍骨﹐牡 蠣 。

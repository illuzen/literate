Shenxu , Kidney Deficient 腎虛

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Shenxu is a term used in Traditional Chinese medicine. It literally means kidney deficient; but it has nothing to do with the organ, kidneys.

Symptoms include weakness and soreness of lower back, weakness in knees, pain in heels, losing hair, bad teeth, tinnitus, spermatorrhea, impotence, irregular menses, dizziness, blurred vision and premature aging.

When yangxu condition also exits, it is called shenyangxu.

When yinxu condition also exits, it is called shenyinxu.

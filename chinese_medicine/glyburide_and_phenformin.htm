glyburide and phenformin

 

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

 

 

glyburide C23H28ClN3O5S

A sulfonylurea  drug being used for lowering of blood sugar of diabetes sufferers.

 

phenformin C 10H 15N 5

A toxic drug  formerly used to treat diabetes but now banned because of its life-threatening side effects

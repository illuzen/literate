Actions & Indications:

主治：

For

  • 1. rose-colored skin rash caused by diseases such as measles, syphilis, or scarlet fever and toxic heat,

  • 2. skin eruption due to wind-heat, other skin and external diseases,

  • 3. excessive thirst,

  • 4. nose bleeding and vomiting of blood.

  • 5. see today's applications

Medical Function:

藥理：

  • 1. anti micro organism,

  • 2. anti cancer,

  • 3. effect on immune system,

  • 4. anti inflammation, and antipyretic

  • inhibition of platelet aggregation: ban lan gen significantly inhibited platelet aggregation induced by adenosine diphosphate on domesticated rabbits. Uridine, hypoxanthine, pyrimidine, salicylic acid that separated from ban lan gen also exhibited some inhibitory activity on adenosine triphosphate (ATP)-induced platelet aggregation  in concentration of  0.21mg/kg. Inhibition rates were 18.2%, 24.2%, 10.8% and 20% respectively..

  • 5. effect on heart and blood vessel

  • 6. liver protection

  • 7. effect on smooth muscle

  • 8. antivirus

  1. 抗微生物

  2. 抗癌

  3. 免疫作用

  4. 抗炎，退 热

  5. 抑制血小板聚集作用: 板藍根對腺苷二磷酸Adenosine diphosphate誘導的家兔血小板聚集有顯著抑制作用．從板藍根中分得尿苷、次黃嘌呤、嘧啶、水楊酸等對ATP (adenosine triphosphate) 誘導的家兔血小板聚集也都表現一定的抑制活性，在0.21mg/kg濃度時其抑制率分別為18.2%、24.2%、10.8%和20%．

  6. 對心血管的作用 ：
    能使離體兔耳和大鼠下肢灌流量增加，改善家兔腸系膜微循環，
    降低麻醉家兔的血壓，
    減少毛細血管通透性，
    使小鼠心肌耗氧量下降．

  7. 對平滑肌 作用

  8. 抗病毒

  9. 解毒作用 有報道認為， 犬用板藍根、黃連粉與藜蘆同服（各2.0g/kg），能解藜蘆毒， 降低死亡率；若藜蘆中毒後再用之，則無效；分別單用板藍根粉或黃連粉， 效果亦不好

Dosage:

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例：

Acute hepatitis with jaundice: ban lan gen 30 g, zhi zi root 45 g. Make into concoction. Total patients: 53. Result: all healed. Duration of therapy: 10 to 50 days [7]

Cautions:

禁忌：

Not to use in absence of fire toxin or in weak patients.

There are report of prolong usage can cause internal bleeding and injury to blood producing function.  There are reports that injectiones have caused allergic reactions and blood in urine.

Allergic reactions included: dizziness, rash, purple lips and face,  numbness in the limbs and in serious condition, shock.

Large dosages and/or long term usage can be toxic to kidneys.

大劑量或長期 服用可能對腎 臟有毒。

wu zhua long 五 爪  龍 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

A manuscript... writing and translation in progress

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Herba Iponoeae

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Ipomoea cairica (L.) Sweet

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

ng5 zaau2 lung4

Other Names:

別名﹕

qi ye qian niu hua 槭葉牽牛花

Common Name:

英文名﹕

Cairo morning glory

Distribution:

產地﹕

Shandong, Jiangxu, Zhejiang, Jiangxi, Hunan, Guangxi, Guangdong, Guizhou, Sichuan etc.

山 東 ﹐ 江 蘇﹐ 浙江、江西、湖南、廣 西 ﹐ 廣 東﹐ 貴 州 、四川等地。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

bitter, sour, cold.

苦、酸、寒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

spleen, liver, lung, large intestine, bladder.

脾、肝、肺、大腸﹐ 膀胱。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 


anti tumor effect

decoction of 31mg/ml can inhibit the growth of leptospirosis.

decoction of wu zhao long can inhibit

decocition of wu zhao long can inhibit bacteri in vitro: Saphylococcus aureus, Hemolytic streptococcus, dysentery bacilli, Escherichia coli.

（1）抗肿瘤的治疗作用。

（2）煎劑 31毫克/毫升，能抑制鉤 端螺旋體 的 生 長 。

（3）煎劑 能 抑制體 外的  金黄色葡萄球菌、溶血性鏈 球菌、痢疾杆菌、大腸 杆菌之 作用。
 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

For treatment of lung heat type of cough, difficulty in urination, gonorrhea, blood in urine, tumors.

治肺熱 咳嗽，小便不利，淋病，尿血，痈疽腫 毒。

外敷癰疽腫毒。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

 

Dosage:

用量﹕

for decoction: 9 to 20 g; or smash and extract the juice for drinking. In powder form: 6 to 18 g. External use: use appropriate amount.

煎劑 ：9~20克，亦用搗 汁服。散劑：6~18克。外用：適 量 。

現代研究﹕

 

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

Not to use in cold and yangxu (yang deficient) type of body.

陽虛者禁服。

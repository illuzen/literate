yíng xiāng LI20 迎香穴   (in English and Chinese 中英文)
 

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu 朱興國編譯

 

yíng xiāng LI 20

 

Location: about one centimeter in the wrinkles beside the nose (in the outer edge of the midpoint of the nose, in the fold of nasolabial).


Indications: rhinitis, nasal congestion, sinusitis, runny nose, nasal, toothache, colds, ascariasis, constipation,  trigeminal neuralgia... Pressing the ying xiang points, can quickly relieve pain of upper teeth.

 

迎香穴 LI 20

定位：在鼻翼旁開約一厘米皺紋中（在鼻翼外緣中點旁，當鼻唇溝中）。

主治： 鼻炎，鼻塞，鼻竇炎，流鼻水，鼻病，牙痛，感冒，膽道蛔蟲病，便秘，臉部神經麻痺等。上齒牙痛時，指壓該穴，可以快速止痛。

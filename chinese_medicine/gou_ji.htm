gou ji 狗脊

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 Rhizoma Cibotii Barometz

Botanical name:

學名﹕

 Cibotium barometz (Linn.) J. Sm.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 kuseki

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 kuch'ok

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

 gau2 zik3

Other Names:

別名﹕

 jin mao gou ji,  金 毛 狗 脊

Common Name:

英文名﹕

 chain fern rhizome, cibota, cibotum

Distribution:

產地﹕

 Fujian, Sichuan, Yunnan, Zhejiang

福 建 ﹐ 四 川 ﹐  雲  南 ﹐  浙 江 。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 bitter, sweet, warm.

苦 ﹐ 甘 ﹐  溫 。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

 kidney, liver.

腎 ﹐ 肝 。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

  

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕
  1. achy loin and knees due to liver and shenxu (kidney deficient)
  2. rheumatoid arthritis due to liver and shenxu (kidney deficient)  
  3. loss of bladder control (gatism)

 

  1. 腎虛肝不足﹐腰膝痠痛。
  2. 腎虛肝不足﹐濕痺痛。
  3. 腎虛肝不足﹐ 小便失禁 。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

  

Dosage:

用量﹕

 4.5 - 9 g in decoction.

4.5 ~ 9 克 。 煎服。

Samples of formulae

:處方舉例﹕
  1. achy loin and knees due to liver and shenxu (kidney deficient) : combine with tu xi zi, niu xi, du zhong etc.
  2. rheumatoid arthritis due to liver and shenxu (kidney deficient)  : combine with gui zhi, qin jiu, hai feng teng, niu xi etc.
 
  1. 腎虛肝不足 腰膝痠痛，常與菟絲子、牛膝、杜仲等品配伍。
  2. 腎虛肝不足 風濕痺痛,  常與桂枝、秦艽、海風藤、牛膝等配伍應用。

 

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

  

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

 not to use in cases of difficulty in urination or heat from yinxu (yin deficient)

小 便 不 利 ﹐ 或 陰虛 有 熱 者 禁忌 用 。

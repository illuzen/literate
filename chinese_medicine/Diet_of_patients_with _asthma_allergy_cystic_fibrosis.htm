Diet of Patients with Asthma, Allergy, Cystic Fibrosis

 

 Edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu   

 

Food may have to avoid:

  1. food high in sugar

  2. food that can turn into sugar fast

  3. food treated with high heat

  4. cured meat

  5. cured fish

  6. stale fish, stale meat

  7. cured eggs

  8. food high in animal fat

Food in question, depending on medications, body syndromes (based on traditional Chinese medicine point of view):

  1. food high in sodium

  2. food high in potassium

  3. food classified as cool food

  4. food classified as warm food

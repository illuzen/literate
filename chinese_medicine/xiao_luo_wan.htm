xiao luo wan 消瘰丸

translated by Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Name of Formula: xiao luo wan

Application: Scrofula, swollen lymph nodes due to infection.

Composition:

xuan shen, mu li, bei mu,  equal amount of each.

Make into powder and make into pills with cooked and condensed honey.

Take 9 grams each time. 2 to 3 times a day.

 

方名：消瘰丸《醫學心悟》


主治：瘰歷，痰核，證見咽乾，舌紅，脈弦滑者。有清熱化痰，散結軟堅之功。
 

組成：玄 參，牡蠣，貝母，等份。煉蜜為丸。

 

每服 9 克。每日2～3次。

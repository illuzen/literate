zhu ling san 豬苓散 (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

 

Name of Formula: zhu ling san

Application: vomiting, ascites, desire to drink water after vomiting

Composition: zhu ling, fu ling, bai zhu in equal amount.

Make into powder.

Use 5 g each time in boiling water. Let cool. Take  3 times a day.

 

方名﹕豬苓散

 

主治：嘔吐，膈上有停飲，吐後欲飲水。

 

組成﹕

豬苓 茯苓 白朮 各等分為末。

每次用5 克。沸水沖。 放涼。 每日服三次。

wu shao she xiao feng yin 烏梢蛇消風飲

 

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu       (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

Name of formula: wu shao she xiao feng yin

Application: chronic eczema or urticaria

Composition:

wu shao she 3 g (ground),  can tui12 g, dang dui15 g, chi shao12 g, fang feng 10 g, jing jie10 g, di fu zi 18 g, chai hu 6 g, bai ji li10 g, gan cao 3 g.

 

Boil with water and use as tea.

 

藥方名：烏梢蛇消風飲

主治：慢性濕疹、蕁麻疹:

烏梢蛇 3克（研末）、 蟬蛻12克、當 歸15克、赤芍12克、防風10克、荊芥10克、

地膚子18克、柴胡 6克、白蒺藜10克、甘草 3克。

水煎服。

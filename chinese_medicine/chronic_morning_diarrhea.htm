Chronic Morning Diarrhea     

Edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Name of Formula: jiang huo sheng shi tang (Nei Wai Shang Bian Huo Lun)

 

Diagnostics:  

For primarily pixu and secondarily shenyangxu  

 

Prescription:

jiang huo, du huo 6 grams each; gao ben, fang feng, zhi gan cao, chuan xiong 3 grams each; man jing zi 2 grams. Boil herbs with water. Take as tea.

 

Adjustment to the formula:

  1. If the diagnosis is shenyangxu, the treatment should be using warming and astringent formula.

  2. If the diagnosis is primarily pixu and secondarily shenyangxu, then  add the following to the formula:   bai zhi, sheng ma, ge gen, cang zhu, bai zhu, bai shao.

 

Adjust formula according to diagnosis.

 

 

Or the following formula can also be selected:

 

bu gu zhi 15 g, da zao 15 g, rou dou kou 5 g, sheng jiang 10 g.

 

Add 800 cc of water. Simmer till 300 cc. 

Make into 2 servings. Take in the morning and evening.

 

 

Source of formula: 內外傷辨惑論

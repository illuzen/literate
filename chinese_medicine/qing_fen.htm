qing fen 輕 粉 （in English and Chinese 中英文)
 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 Calomelas

Chemical name:

化學名﹕

 Calomeo mercurous chloride (Hg2Cl2),

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 kaifun

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 kyongbun

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

 hing1 fan2

Other Names:

別名﹕

gong fen (mercury powder)

汞粉

qiao fen (precipitous powder)

峭粉

shui yin fen (mercury powder)

水銀粉

ni fen (soft powder)

膩粉

yin fen (silver powder)

銀粉

sao pen

掃盆

Common Name:

英文普通名﹕

 Calomel (mercurous chloride)

Distribution:

產地﹕

 Mainly in Hubei, Hunan, Hebei, Yunnan province of China..

主產於湖北，湖南，河北，雲南。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 acrid, cold, dry and strong, toxic

辛，寒，燥烈有毒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

 large intestine

大 腸。
Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

  

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕
  1. use externally to kill parasites; for ringworms, boils and syphilis.
  2. take internally to promote urination and bowel movements. Usually use with other water-removing herbs in difficulty in urination and defecation.

外用攻毒殺虫：用於疥，癬，梅毒。

內服利水通便：用於水腫鼓脹，二便不利者，配逐水藥。
 

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

mercury chloride   (Hg2Cl2 or HgCl)

氯 化亞汞或氯化汞

Dosage:

用量﹕

 0.1 to 0.2 g taken in pills or powder form. Use appropriate amount for external application.

内服 0.1～0.2g，入丸散。外用適量。

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

  

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

  

Cautions:

禁忌﹕

  do not overdose and not for long term use. After taking this herb, rinse the mouth immediately to prevent injury to the mouth. Not to be used in case of pregnancy.

內服不可過量久用。 服後須及時漱口﹐以免口腔糜爛。 孕婦忌用。

dan fan san   蛋礬散

translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Name of Formula: dan fan san

Application: leukemia

Composition:

chicken egg shells (bake till yellowish) 60 g, liao jiang shi, zao fan 50 g,

 red ren shen  30 g, shan ci gu 50 g.

Make into fine powder. Put into capsules. Take 1 to 3 g

each time, 3 times per day, with warm boiled drinking water.

Luan Fa Ji Zi Gao 亂髮雞子膏

Translated by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu  （in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

Name of formula:

Luan Fa Ji Zi Gao (human hair egg yolk cream) (Source: Zhuan Xin Fang)

Application: heat sores or herpes simple I of little children

Prescription: egg yolks 5 pieces, human hair a handful about  size of an egg.

Place the 2 items on an iron frying pan and heat. At first it seems very dry. After a while liquid will come out (fat from egg yolk). Then put into a china bowl. Smear on the boils, sprinkle ku shen powder over it.

Also see: wu wei xiao du yin, an oral formula

藥方名：亂髮雞子膏 （劉禹錫《傳信方》

治孩子熱瘡。

熱瘡是急性疱疹性皮膚病。其主要徵狀為皮膚黏膜交界處發生成簇水泡、糜爛、潰破、結痂，疼痛相兼。

處方：雞子五枚（去白取黃），亂髮如雞子許大。二味相和於鐵銚子中，炭火熬，初甚干，少頃即發焦，遂有液出(蛋黃之脂肪)，旋取置一瓷碗中，以液盡為度，取塗熱瘡上，即以苦參末粉之。

亦看 五味消毒飲

betulin    白樺脂醇    (in English and Chinese 中英文)
 

Translated from Chinese by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

 

Betulin
Synonyms:Trochol; betulol; betuline; betulinol;betula camphor.
Molecular Formula: C30H50O2
Molecular weight: 442.7
Melting point : 256-257 C
White powder
Solubility: soluble in alcohol, ether, chloroform and benzene, slightly soluble in cold water, petroleum ether.
Plant sources: white birch bark extract.
Betulin has the effect of anti-inflammatory, anti-virus, inhibition of protein dissolving  of the hair and improving damaged lustre of hair, and promote hair growth and other activity.

Uses: being used in food, cosmetics and medicine.
 

白樺脂醇

別名：白樺酯醇，樺木醇，樺木腦。

分子式：C30H50O2

分子 量：442.7

熔點：256-257 C

白色粉末

溶解性：溶於乙醇，乙醚，氯仿和苯，微溶於冷水，石油醚等。

植物來源：白樺樹皮提取。

白樺脂醇具有消炎，抗病毒，抑制頭髮纖維中蛋白質溶解，改善受損頭髮光澤，促進頭髮生長等活性。

 

用途： 應用於食品，化妝品及醫藥等。

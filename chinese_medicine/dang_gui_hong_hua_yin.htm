dang gui hong hua yin 當歸紅花飲

Translated and edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Name of Formula: dang gui hong hua yin

Application: measles about to ripen but unable to ripen, or color of the measles is dark due toxue re (heat in blood) andxue yu (blood stasis) .

Composition: dang gui, hong hua, niu bang zi, lian qiao, ge gen,  gan cao.

藥方名：當歸紅花飲《麻科活人全書》

主治：疹出而復收，或血熱血淤，斑疹色暗者。

组成：當 歸、紅 花、牛蒡子、連翹、葛 根、甘 草。

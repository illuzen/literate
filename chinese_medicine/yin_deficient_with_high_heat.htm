yinxu (yin deficient) with high heat 陰虚火旺

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu    (in English and Chinese 中英文)         

Syndrome of yinxu (yin deficient) with fire:

Priapism (constant erection of penis), spermatorrhea, dizziness and tiredness, insomnia, lots of dream, achy and lack of strength in loin and knees area, uncomfortably hot chest, palms of hands, and feet, dry mouth with instant sweating during sleep, red tongue with scanty fur, thin and rapid pulse.

陰虚火旺症狀：

陰莖強硬不倒，精液自流，頭暈神疲，失眠多夢，腰膝酸軟，五心煩熱，口乾盜汗，舌紅少苔，脈細數。

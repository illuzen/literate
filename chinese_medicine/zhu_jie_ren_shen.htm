zhu jie ren shen; zhu jie shen 竹節人参，竹節参

 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

A manuscript... writing and translation in progress

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Rhizoma Panacis japonici
 

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Panax japonicus C. A. Mey.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

zuk1 zit3 sam1

Other Names:

別名﹕

zhu jie shen 竹節参

Common Name:

英文名﹕

Japanese Ginseng

Distribution:

產地﹕

 

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

sweat, slightly bitter, warm

甘、微苦，溫。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

liver, spleen, lung

歸肝、脾、肺經。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Being used as tonic, as analgesics, and to arrest bleeding, to rid of clot

blood. Clinically being used in deficiency after sickness, chronic cough with

blood, cough with abundance of phlegm, injuries from impact.

滋補強壯，散淤止痛，止血祛痰。用於病後虛弱，勞嗽咯血，咳嗽痰多，

跌扑損傷。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

 

Dosage:

用量﹕

6 to 9 g.

6～9克。

thrombocytopenia

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Any of the bleeding disorders that result from a lack of functional platelets (thrombocytes), including:

  1. idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura (ITP) (primary thrombocythemia)

  2. thrombotic thrombocytopenic purpura

  3. drug induced immune thrombocytopenia

  4. drug induced non-immune thrombocytopenia

  5. acquired platelet function defect

  6. congenital platelet function defects

  7. acute myelocytic leukemia

Complementary and alternative therapies:

　

  1. qigong therapy,

  2. Chinese medicine therapy for ITP

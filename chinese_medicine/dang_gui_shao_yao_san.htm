dang gui shao yao san 當歸芍藥散

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu     Click here for Chinese. 按此看中文。

Name of Formula: dang gui shao yao san (Source: Rui Zhu Tang Jing Yan Fang)

Applications:

  • pregnancy with tension in the and ache in the abdomen.

  • irregular menses, blurry in the eyes, dizziness, tiredness, pain in abdomen, achy lower back.

  • pink and white vagina discharge in bacterial and yeast infection

Prescription:

shao yao 15 g, dang gui 12 g, fu ling 15 g, bai zhu 15 g, ze xie  15g, chuan xiong 12 g.

Add 3 bowls of water. Boil down to 90% of a bowl.

Drink an hour before meals while warm.

當歸芍藥散 (瑞竹堂經驗方)

主治﹕婦人妊娠﹐腹中拘急﹐綿綿作痛。月經不順﹐

頭暈目眩﹐疲勞倦怠﹐腹痛腰酸。赤白帶下。

處方﹕

芍藥 四 錢﹐  當 歸  三 錢 ﹐茯 苓 四 錢﹐白朮 四 錢﹐ 澤瀉  四 錢﹐  川 芎 三 錢

水 三 碗 煎 九分。 飯前一小時溫服 。

註：舊制一錢 = 3.75克。

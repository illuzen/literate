ji xue teng 雞血藤

 

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Herb

圖片來源﹕:中國科普博覽會植物館雞血藤莖葉 

 Plant

Present Day Applications:

現代應用﹕

ji xue teng syrup is being used in low platelet count and getting some result..

近代以雞血藤糖漿治白血球細胞減少症有一定療效.

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Radix et Caulis Jixueteng

Biological name:

拉丁文學名﹕

Millettia Dielsiana Harms  (香花岩豆藤 (山雞血藤).)

Millettia reticulata Benth , (昆明雞血藤)

Spatholobus suberectus Dunn (密花豆屬密花豆)

Kadsura interior A.c.Smith  (内南五味子)

Kadsura heteroclita (Roxb.) Craib．(異形南五味子)

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

keiketto

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

kyehyoltung

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

gai1 hyut3 tang4

Other Names:

別名﹕

feng xue teng 風血藤,  ma lu teng 馬鹿藤,  zi geng teng 紫梗藤.

Common Name:

英文名﹕

Diel's millettia (Millettia Dielsiana)

 (Spatholobus suberectus)

Distribution:

產地﹕

Guangdong, Guangxi, Yunnan provinces of China.

分 佈 於 廣 東 ﹐ 廣 西﹐ 雲南  。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

bitter, slightly sweet, warm and nontoxic.

味苦﹐微甘，性溫﹐無毒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

heart, spleen, liver.

歸心﹐脾﹐肝經。　　

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

Medical functions:

  1. Decoction of Millettia Dielsiana Harms   (山雞血藤) or Spatholobus suberectus Dunn (密花豆), showed that it could improve the anemia condition (created in laboratory) of rabbits and possessed anti-inflammatory effect.

  2. Small amount can enhance the rhythmic contraction of the uteri of rabbits, and in larger amount the effect is even stronger. Conceived uteri are sensitive.

  3. Decoction of Millettia Dielsiana (山雞血藤) can inhibit Staphylococcus aureus

  4. Decoction of Millettia Dielsiana (山雞血藤) enhance the function of macrophages and improve immune system.

  5. Inhibits platelet coagulation caused by adenosine diphosphate (in vitro), thus lower the vaso-resistance and improved the circulation of the coronary arteries and the circulation in the thigh.

  6. improved the low white cell count of lab mice caused by  cyclophosphamide  (環磷醯胺).

  7. lowered total cholesterol and increased the low density cholesterol of quails

藥理﹕

  1. 密花豆及山雞血藤煎劑對實驗性貧血的家兔均有補血作用，有抗炎作用。

  2. 小劑量能增強子宮節律性收縮，較大劑量收縮更顯著，已孕子宮較未孕子宮敏感。

  3. 山雞血藤煎劑體外試驗對金黃葡萄球菌有抑制作用。

  4. 增強巨噬細胞功能。增強免功能。

  5. 對環磷醯胺所致的小白鼠白血球細胞下降有顯著的治療作用。

  6. 在體外能抑制二磷腺甘或膠原誘導的血小板聚集。降低血管阻力，如能增加離體免心冠脈血流量﹐股動脈的血流量等。

  7. 降低鹌鹑的總胆固醇，提高高密度胆固醇：

  8. 抑制心脏與降低血压作用

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Actions & Indications:
  1. irregular menses, flow of menses not smooth, menostasis due to blood deficient  ,
  2. suitable for diseases due to blood deficient or blood stasis   .
  3. low platelet count
  4. rheumatoid arthritis, especially with blood deficient and blood stasis.
  5. seguelae of strokes; combine other herbs with blood and qi tonic properties.
  6. blood deficient; combine other herbs with qi and blood tonic properties.

 

  1. 用於月經不調﹐經行不暢﹐血虛經閉等症。
  2. 治白血球細胞減少症。
  3. 雞血藤既能活血，又能補血，對 血瘀﹐血虛 之症均適用。
  4. 風濕性關節炎，尤其是慢性患者有血虛血瘀的更為適宜。用於風濕痺痛及手足麻木﹐肢體癱瘓﹐血虛萎黃等，配祛風濕藥同用。
  5. 治中風後肢體癱瘓，配益氣養血活血通絡藥同用。
  6. 用於血虛萎黃，則配補益氣血藥同用

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

 
jixuetengsterol雞血藤醇
organic acid有機酸
tannin acid鞣質
friedelin無羈
friedelinsterol無羈醇

β- Sitosterolβ-谷甾醇
stigmasterol豆甾醇
campesterol菜油固醇
calcium oxalate草酸鈣

Dosage:

用量﹕

10 to 20 g. Concentrate extract 0.5 ~ 2 g.

10～20克；濃縮中藥：0.5～2 克。

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

For blood stasis: ji xue teng, hong hua, chuan xiong , xiang fu

For blood deficient: ji xue teng,  shu di huang, dang gui.

瘀滯者，配 紅花﹐川 芎﹐香附子﹐等以活血化瘀調經。

血虛者，配 熟地黃﹐當 歸, 等以養血調經。

Cautions:

禁忌﹕

Use with care for those with yinxu with fire syndrome.

 陰虚火旺者慎用。

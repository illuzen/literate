shi xiao san   失笑散           (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

Translated by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

 

Name of Formula: shi xiao san (Source of formula: Tai Ping Hui Min He Ji Ju Fang)

 

Application: blood stasis. Syndromes: great pain in chest and abdomen, irregular menses, acute pain in the lower abdomen,  difficulty in discharge of clot blood after giving birth.

 

Composition: wu ling zhi 12ｇ pu huang 12ｇ

 

失 笑 散  《太平惠民和剤局方 》
組成：五靈脂12ｇ 蒲黃12ｇ
功用：活血祛瘀，散結止痛。
主治：血淤停滯。
特征：心腹巨痛，或產後悪露不行，或月經不調，少腹急痛等。

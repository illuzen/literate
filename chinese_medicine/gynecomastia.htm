Gynecomastia  男性乳房過度長大症       (In English and Chinese 中英文)

Edited by : Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Excessive development of the male breasts because of over development of mammary glands. Sometimes the function of female mammary glands can also appear. A common cause of gynecomastia is the increased ratio of estradiol and testosterone in older men. Temporary enlargement of the breasts is not unusual or abnormal in boys during adolescence or during recovery from malnutrition. Gynecomastia may be abnormal as, for example, in Klinefelter's syndrome.

男性乳房過度長大是因為乳腺過度增生。有時女性乳腺功能有時也可能會出現。老年男性乳房發育症的常見原因是雌激素和睪丸激素的增加比例。男孩在青春期或在恢復營養不良過程中，臨時擴大的乳房是常見的。乳房發育可能是異常，例如：克氏綜合徵。

有男子服用當歸幾個月後乳房變大的病例。停止服用後乳房回復原狀。

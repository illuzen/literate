Chinese Chronological System

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

     The chronological system of  Chinese history is based on the Dynasties of China and the years of the individual emperors of the different dynasties. It is difficult to accurately translate into the western system which is based on the years of Christ. This might seem strange to westerners but using the years of one person  (Christ) or using the years of different emperors is based on the same logical (or illogical) thinking.

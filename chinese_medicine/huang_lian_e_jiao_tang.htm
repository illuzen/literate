huang lian e jiao tang  黃連阿膠湯   (in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu       按此看中文

Name of Formula: huang lian e jiao tang （Shang Han Lun)

Diagnosis:

Heart fire is high , yinxu ( yin deficient),  tongue coating is yellow and tip is red,  rapid thin pulse, over-worked mentally, restlessness and insomnia, dry mouth, boils in the mouth and tongue, frequent dream ,

forgetfulness, neurasthenia.

Present day application: insomnia, schizophrenia, bleeding disorder, pneumonia, enteritis, erysipelas, scarlet fever, meningitis, cerebral hemorrhage, hypertension,

psoriasis, itchy skin.

Composition:

 huang lian 1 1/2 qian, ,  huang qin 3 qian, bai shao 4 qian, e jiao 3 qian, chicken egg yolks 2 pieces.  Add water and boil all herbs except  e jiao. After the decoction is ready, strain and then add e jiao and dissolve it in the liquid.  Make into 2 servings.  Add one chicken egg yolk to each serving while the liquid is hot.

1 qian = 3.75 grams (old measurement)

黃連阿膠湯 《傷寒論 》
主治：滋陰清心。適用於用陰虛而腦過度。證見﹕
心煩不眠，五心煩熱，口乾津少，口舌生瘡，多夢健忘，
腦神經衰弱。現代應用於：睡眠障礙，精神分裂症，諸出血症，
肺炎，腸炎，丹毒，猩紅熱，腦膜炎，腦溢血，高血壓，乾癬，皮膚搔癢症。
 
處方：
黃連錢半﹐阿膠三錢 沖服﹐黃芩三錢﹐芍藥四錢。
先煎黃連, 黃芩, 芍藥。  過濾後﹐ 再溶化阿膠入藥汁內，
加雞蛋黃沖服. 
分二次服用。服時每服藥加雞蛋黃一枚。

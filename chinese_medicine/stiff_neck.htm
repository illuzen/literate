Torticollis 

 

 by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu    按 此 看 中 文

 

Simple torticollis should be differentiated from atlantoaxial subluxation.

atlantoaxial subluxation

High incidences among children of 2 - 12 years old.

 

Causes of atlantoaxial subluxation includes:

 

  • arthritic

    1. rheumatoid arthritis

    2. juvenile rheumatoid arthritis

    3. psoriatic arthritis

    4. ankylosing spondylitis (chronic inflammationof the spine and the sacroiliac joints)

    5. systemic lupus (SLE)

       

  • congenital

    1. Down syndrome (20%)

    2. Morquio disease

    3. atlanto-occipital fusion

    4. congenital absence/hypoplasia of dens (2/3 have h/o trauma)

       

  • infectious

    1. retropharyngeal abscess

       

  • traumatic

jing lian fen (brighten face powder) 瑩臉粉

 by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

 

 

Name of Formula: jing lian fen (source of formula: Ben Cao Gang Mu)

Application: for brown spots on the face

Compositon: bai fu zi, dong gua zi, bai ji, shi liu pi. Use equal amount and make into fine powder. Soak in rice wine for 3 days. Apply to face after washing.  

 

藥方名：瑩臉粉 《本 草綱目》

主治：治 面黑不白或面部黑褐斑。

組成 ：

白附子﹐冬瓜子﹐白及﹐石榴皮等份為末，酒浸三日，洗臉後 塗之，久則令臉瑩如玉。

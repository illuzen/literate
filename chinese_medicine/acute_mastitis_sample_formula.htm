early stage acute mastitis, a sample formula      (in English and Chinese 中英文)
 

 初期急性乳腺炎

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

 

For treatment of early stage acute mastitis:

Composition:

 

jin yin hua 24 g, pu gong ying 15 g, lian qiao 9 g, chen pi 9 g, qing pi 6 g, raw gan cao 9 g.

 

Boil with water twice. Make into 2 servings. One dose per day. If sickness is serious, 2 dosages per day can be taken. (Middle Medical Journal, (3)：167，1964)

 

The formula is an example. If you are a patient, consult your healthy care provider.

 

See causes and prevention.

 

 初期急性乳腺炎

 

處方：

金銀花八錢，蒲公英五錢，連翹、陳皮各三錢，青皮、生甘草各二錢。上為一劑量，水煎二次，並分二次服，每日一劑，嚴重者可一日服兩劑。（《中級醫刊》（3）：167，1964）

　

此藥方，只是作專業者參考用。如你是乳腺炎患者，請看醫生。

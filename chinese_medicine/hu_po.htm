hu po 琥珀

 

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu   

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Succinum

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

kohaku

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

hoback

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

fu2 paak3

Other Names:

別名﹕

xue po 血珀

Common Name:

英文名﹕

fossil of amber

Distribution:

產地﹕

Mainly produced in Yunnan, Guangxi, Fujian, Guizhou, Liaoning, Xinjiang, Guangdong provinces and also in Southeast Asia.

主產雲南，廣西，福建，貴州，遼 寧，新疆，廣東，等 省，及 南洋諸島。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

sweet, neutral.

甘，平。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

heart, liver, bladder.

入心，肝，膀胱經。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Uses: rid of blood clots, to promote calming, to promote urination, for treatment of epilepsy, insomnia, blood in urine, menses stoppage due to blood stasis and clots .

功用﹕散瘀止血；鎮驚安神﹐利水通淋。主 治﹕驚風癲癇，驚悸﹐失眠；治血便血尿﹐利尿，血瘀所致的 閉經。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

 
succoxyabietic acid琥珀氧松香酸
borneol ether龍腦醚
Succinic acid琥珀酸
succinoabietinolic acid琥珀松香酸
succinosilvic acid琥珀銀松酸
succinoresinol琥珀脂醇
succinoabietol琥珀松香醇
benzine石油醚
carbon, hydrogen, oxygen, small amount of sulfur, iron, magnesium, calcium etc.碳，氫，氧，少許硫，鐵，鎂，鈣等。

Daly Dosage:

每日用量﹕

1. 5 g to 3 g, in powder form taken with liquid. Not used in decoction.

一克半 至三克。研末沖服。不入 煎劑。

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

See: Interstitial Cystitis.  看間質性膀胱炎

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

 

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

do not use in case of yinxu (yin deficient)  with heat.

陰虛有熱忌用。

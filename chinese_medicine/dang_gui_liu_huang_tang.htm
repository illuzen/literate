dang gui liu huang tang 當歸六黃湯

Edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu       按此看中文。

Name of Formula: dang gui liu huang tang

Application: yinxu (yin deficient)  with heat: fever, night sweating, red face, dry mouth, uncomfortable, constipation, dark red urine.

Composition:

dang gui 9 g,  sheng di huang 15 g, shu di huang 12 g, huang qin 9 g,

huang lian 8 g, huang bai 8 g, huang qi 15 g.

Make into decoction.

Adjustments:

Yellowish vaginal discharge：add  che qian zi, fu ling, huai shan

Tuberculosis with hot flashes：add zhi mu, gui ban, bie jia

blood deficient ：add bai shao, dan shen

Sweating is heavy: add long gu,  mu li, wu wei zi

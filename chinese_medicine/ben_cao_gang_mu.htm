Ben Cao Gang Mu (Materia Medica  Outline)﹐本草綱目,  is one of the most important Chinese medical material books, written by Li Shi zhen  (1518-1593)。It consists of 52 volumes, with more than 1.9 million characters and more than one thousand and one hundred pictures. The book lists 1,892 medical material of herbs, animals and mineral, 374 of which were new additions from the then known species, with11,096 formulae being used in the past. It has more than 60 different editions. The book has been translated into different languages: Japanese, French, German, Russian, Latin, etc..

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

The active chemical ingredient is picrotoxin which is an antagonist for GABA A receptor. It can excite the CNS.

印度防己之有效成份毛果芸香鹼有抗 γ- 氨基丁 酸 A (GABA A) 受體之作用。能興奮中區神經。

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Being used in homeopathy for:

  1. nausea, vomiting, headache, vertigo, when traveling in carriage, car, airplane or ship;

  2. insomnia

  3. dyspepsia (indigestion)

  4. epilepsy

  5. leucorrhea (vagina white discharge, yeast infection)

  6. dymenorrhea (painful menses)

在順勢療法用於：

  1. 舟車暈浪頭暈作嘔，嘔吐，

  2. 失眠

  3. 消化不良

  4. 癲癇症
  5. 白帶
  6. 經痛

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

Not to use during pregnancy, in small children or if the body is weak.

The active ingredient of Coccolus indica is picrotoxin. It is a neuro toxin. Do not over dose.

孕婦、小兒、體虛者禁內服。

印度防己有效成份是毛果芸香鹼。它是一種神經毒素。不可多用。

qun di cai 裙蔕菜

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu    朱興國編 譯     (in English and Chinese 中英文)

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 Thallus undariae

Botanical name:

拉丁生物學名﹕

undaria pinnatifida (Sur.) Harriott.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 wakame

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 gun che chae （miyeok)

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

 kwan4 dai3 coi3

Other Names:

別名﹕

  

Common Name:

英文名﹕

 sea mustard;  also called by the Japanese as wakame in America.

Distribution:

產地﹕

 being produced in Japan, China (especially in Dalian), Korea. It is also found in south-eastern New Zealand and Australia.

在日本，中國 (尤其在大連)，韓國有種植。澳洲，纽西蘭的東南部也有發現。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 salty, cold.

鹹，寒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕
Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 Anti viral effect: the active chemical ingredient ofgalactofucan is significantly more active against clinical strains of HSV-2 than HSV-1, p < 0.001.[1]

Digestibility and retention coefficients for calcium, magnesium, zinc, iron, sodium and potassium were lower for seaweed-fed rats, and showed lower values for Wakame (undaria pinnatifida) than Nori. (Porphyra tenera)[2]

抗病毒作用：裙蔕菜有效成份褐藻糖胶對疱疹病毒有抑制作用。對疱疹病毒 II 型比對疱疹病毒 I 型更有效。【1】

喂吃海草的大鼠吸收消化鈣，鎂，鋅，鐵，鈉，鉀比對照組小。喂食裙蔕菜比起喂食紫菜組較小。【2】

化學成份﹕

毒素與禁忌﹕

過度服食裙蔕菜可能引起碘過多 。

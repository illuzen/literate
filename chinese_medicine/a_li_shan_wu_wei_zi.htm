a li shan wu wei zi 阿里山五味子

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu   (in English and Chinese 中英文)

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Fructus schisandra arisanensis

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Schisandra arisanensis Hayata (Schizandra arisanensis)

Common Name:

英文名﹕

　

Distribution:

產地﹕

Taiwan

臺湾

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

warm, sour, astringent, tonic, sedative

温，酸，澀，補，鎮靜

Channels (meridians) entered:

　

歸經﹕

kidney, heart and lung

腎，心，肺。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

　

　

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

For improving  blood circulation, ridding of clots, ridding of swelling and pain, ridding of dampness and improving digestion, ridding of ache and pain from physical exertion, for treating hypertension, inflammation of stomach, diabetes, injuries from impact.

活血祛瘀，疏經活絡，消腫止痛，利濕健胃，治勞力過度，筋骨酸痛，胃熱腹痛，胃炎，高血壓，糖尿病，跌打損傷．

　

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

Schiarisanrin-C

Dosage:

用量﹕

1.5–8 g per day

每日量：1.5 ∼ 8 克。

處方舉例﹕

現代研究﹕

　

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

use with care in patients with excess heat,

有熱者，小心應用。

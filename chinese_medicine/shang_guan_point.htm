shang guan point GB 3 上關穴 GB 3 (in English and Chinese 中英文)
Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu 朱興國編譯

 

Shàng guān（GB3）is on Gallbladder channel of foot shaoyang.

Location: straight up from xia guan point S7 (ST 7) , at the edge of the zygomatic arch.
Application; headache, tinnitus and deafness, toothache, drooping of eyes and mouth, facial pain, epilepsy.

Needle method: straight in,  0.5 to 1 cun

 

上關穴 GB 3為足少陽膽經之穴。
定位；下關穴直上，顴骨弓上緣處。
主治；頭痛，耳鳴耳聾，齒痛，口眼喎斜，臉痛，驚癇。
刺法；直刺 0.5-1 寸。

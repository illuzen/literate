sheng yang chu shi fang feng tang

升陽除濕防風湯

Name of Formula: sheng yang chu shi fang feng tang

Source of Formula: Li Dong Yuan (bi wei lun)

Application: the name: of the formula means fang feng soup for improving body function and rid of infection. For diarrhea with blood, constipation, urgency feeling in the bowel and heaviness feeling at the rectum anus area, may be some mucus or blood is present in stool.

Composition:

cang zhu 10 g, bai zhu 10 g, bai shao 10 g, fu ling 10 g, fang feng  6 g.

Make into decoction.

　

升陽除濕防風湯﹐李東垣﹕《脾胃論腸澼下血論》

主治: 腸澼下血，大便閉塞，或裡急後重，或有少白膿，或少有血。”

組成﹕

 蒼 朮 10 克﹐白 朮 10克﹐ 白 芍 10克﹐茯 苓 10克﹐ 防風 6克。

水煎服。

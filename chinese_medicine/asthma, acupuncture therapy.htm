Asthma, Acupuncture Therapy

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

1. Body acupuncture

Prescription: shan zhong (Ren 17), kong zui (L 6), yu ji (L 10) and fei shu (B 13)

Supplementary points: 

for shi zheng (excess) add feng long (S 40), tian tu (Ren 22) and ding chuan (extra 14).

for xu zheng (deficient) add gao huang shu (B34), qi hai (Ren 6), shen shu (B 23), tai xi (K 3), and zu san li (S 36).

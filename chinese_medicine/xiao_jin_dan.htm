xiao jin dan     

 

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu                   按此看中文

xiao jin dan (Source: wai ke quan sheng ji fang)

Being used for removing phlegm and dampness, ridding of blood stasis, for lymphatic tumor, chronic lymphatic leukemia. Long term usage is effective for bone TB. Do not use during pregnancy.

composition:

  • feng xiang zhi  15 g,

  • cao wu tou  15 g,

  • wu ling zhi 15 g,

  • di long 15 g,

  • tu mu bie, 15 g,

  • ru xiang 7.5 g,

  • mo yao 7.5 g,

  • dang gui, 7.5 g,

  • she xiang 0.3 g,

  • mo tan 0.12 g.

Make into pill. Take 5 g per day.

 

小金丹(外科全生集方)

化痰袪涇，去瘀通絡作用，主治一切痰核流注，貼骨陰疽，治骨結核，淋巴瘤治骨結核， 慢性淋巴細胞性白血病等。

處方﹕楓香脂﹐草烏頭﹐五靈脂﹐地龍﹐土木蟞﹐各15公分﹐

乳香﹐沒藥﹐當歸﹐各7公分半﹐麝香0。3公分﹐墨炭0。12公

分。為丸。每服5公分。陳酒送服。治骨結核。久服有效。

孕婦忌服。

Editor note: tu mu bie, di long, and cao wu tou are toxic. The usage should be supervised by a qualified herb doctor.

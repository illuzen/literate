Herbal formulae for impotence

By: Joe Hing Kwok Chu          按此看中文

Emotional injuries

Clinical symptoms:

Impotence, erection but lacking rigidity, palpitation, timid, sleeping shallowly, unhappy, fullness in chest area, tongue slightly red in color, pulse is xian-xi (stringy and thin).

Formula: qu yin yu xin dan [source: bian zheng lun ]

ren shen 6 g, yuan zhi 2 g, fu shen 9 g, chang pu 6 g, gan cao 6 g, jie hong 3 g,

sha ren 3 g, chai hu 6 g, tu si zi 9 g, bia zhu 9 g, suan zao ren 6 g, dang gui 6 g,

bai shao 9 g, shan yao 9 g, shen qu 6 g

Cook with water. One prescription a month. Make into

two servings. To be taken before meal.

1.

shenxu (kidney channel deficiency) 腎虚陽萎

2.

ming men lacks fire 命門火衰

3.

heart and spleen (meridians) weakened from over work 勞損心脾

4.

emotional injuries 情绪損傷

5.

damp heat settlement 濕熱下注

6.

liver qi stagnation （depression) 肝氣鬱 結

7.

Clot blood obstruction 血淤阻礙

8

shenyinxu (kidney yin deficiency) 腎陰虚

9

sclerosis of the liver    肝硬化

10

hypertension 血壓高

11

Prostate Enlargement 前列腺肥大

主治：陽痿，或起而不堅，心怯，心悸， 睡不安，神志不愉快，胸脅滿悶，舌淡紅，脈絃細。

吉林人參 6克, 遠志 2克, 茯神 9克, 菖蒲 6克, 甘草 6克, 桔紅 3克, 砂仁 3克, 柴胡 6克, 菟絲子 9克, 白術 9克, 酸棗仁 6克, 當歸 6克, 白芍 9克, 山藥 9克, 神曲 6克 。

水煎服。分二次服。每月一劑。

long xian xiang 龍涎香   ( in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu    朱興國編 譯   

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

  

Botanical name:

拉丁生物學名﹕

Source of herb：

Physeter catodon L.;

Physeter macrocephalus L.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 ryusenko

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 yong yeon hyang

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

 lung4 jin4 heong1

Other Names:

別名﹕
long xie 龍泄
long xian 龍涎
long fu xiang 龍腹香
jing xian xiang 鯨涎香

Common Name:

英文名﹕

 ambergris

Distribution:

產地﹕

 all over the ocean.

全球海洋。

Properties

characteristics﹕

性味﹕

 sweet, fishy smell. astringent.

甘，氣腥，味涩。

Channels meridians entered:

 

歸經﹕

 heart, liver, lung, kidney.

心，肝，肺，腎。
Medical functions:

藥理﹕【1】

 

Its function is similar to she xiang. A small amount can excite the central nervous system; and large amount can inhibit CNS. and have the effect of dropping blood pressure.

 龍涎香與麝香相似，小量對動物中樞神經系統有興奮作用，大量則表現抑制；對離體動物的心臟有強心作用，能引起血壓下降。

Actions & Indications:[2]

主治﹕【2】

 化痰平喘；行氣散結；利水通淋。主喘咳氣逆；胸悶氣結；症瘕積聚；心腹疼痛；神昏；淋証。

Phlegm and asthma; promoting circulation and resolving tumors, promoting urination,  for treatment of cough; chest tightness,  lump or tumor in abdomen; chest and abdomen pain, coma; stranguria

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕【1】

The secretion of Physeter macrocephalu contains:

:
25% of ambrein 25%的龍涎香醇
dihydro-γ-ionone 二氫-γ-紫羅壯酮
α-ambrinol α-龍涎香八氫萘醇
ambra-aldehyde 龍涎香醛
8，13-epoxy-14，15，16trinorlabdan-13-ol 8，13-環氧-14，15，16-三去甲半日花烷-13-醇
8，13-epoxy-12，13-dedehydro-14，15，16-trinorlabdane 8，13-環氧-12，-13-二去氫-14，15，16-三去甲半日花烷
coprosterol 糞甾醇
epicoprosterol 表糞甾醇
cholesterol 膽甾醇
calcium and magnesium, phosphorus, copper, gallium, zinc, aluminum, manganese, strontium, niobium, chromium, lanthanum, nickel, titanium, tungsten 鈣鎂、磷、銅、鎵、鋅、鋁、錳、鍶、鈮、鉻、鑭、鎳、鈦、鎢等。

 0.3 g ~ 1 g in powder form.

研末，內服：0.3 克～1克。

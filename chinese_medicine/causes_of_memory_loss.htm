Causes of Memory Loss

 Compiled and Edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

 

Causes:

  1. Alzheimer's disease

  2. Pick's disease

  3. Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease (CJD) caused by prions. Prions are caused by mutated genes or also can cause by prion diseased feed animals like cows or sheep with scrapie.

  4. Parkinson's diseases

  5. Dementia with Lewy bodies (DLB)

  6. Multiple sclerosis (MS)

  7. Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS)

  8. Toxin from shellfish caused by harmful algal bloom (red tide)

  9. Blood stasis (blood clots)

  10. Chronic infection

  11. Depression (mania or depressed mood)

  12. shenyangxu (kidney yang deficient)

  13. shenyinxu (kidney yin deficient) 

  14. Pixu (spleen deficient) causes over active of the enzyme AchE that metabolizes the neurotransmitter, acetylcholine drastically. Pixu (spleen deficient) causes weak digestion, which promotes yeast infection. Chronic yeast infection can cause systemic infection and activate antimicrobial peptide. Beta amyloid, an antimicrobial peptide[1] has been suspected in damaging the dendrites of nerve cells and causes Alzheimer's disease. About AChE, see Huperzia serrata.

  15. Head injuries

  16. low testosterone (men and women)

  17. Tumors in the brain

  18. Normal pressure hydrocephalus (NPH), also called symptomatic hydrocephalus)

  19. Vitamin B12 deficiency

  20. Vitamin D deficiency

  21. Vitamin B1 deficiency

  22. Side effects of medicines or drug inter-actions

  23. Substance abuse including chronic alcohol intoxication  [2].

  24. Encephalitis (infection of the brain)

  25. HIV/AIDS

福建中醫學院學報>16卷2期

 

系統識別號 816971
篇　名脾虛大鼠脑神经生化指标 变化的实验研究
並列篇名

Experimental Study on the Changes of Biochemical Indicators of Cerebral Nerves

in Spleen Deficiency Rats

作　者 鄧月娥(Yue-E Deng);纪 立金(Li-Jin Ji)
刊　名 福建中醫學院學報
卷期／出版年月 16卷2期(2006/04)
頁次 49-50
資料語文 中文
摘要研究中醫脾虛証對大鼠 腦記憶功能生化指標的影響。用瀉下和勞倦法制作脾虛大鼠模型，再用四君子湯治療，檢測大鼠的腦生化指標。結果：脾虛組AchE明 顯升高，與正常組比較差異非常顯著(P<0.01)，與四君子湯治 療組比較．差異顯著(P<0.05)；脾虛組NO含量、NOS活性明顯較正常組及四君子湯治療組低(P<0.05)。 結論：脾虛 影響大鼠腦記憶功能的生化指標。

To investigate the influence of spleen deficiency on memory. The model rats of spleen deficiency were made by diarrhea and fatigue, and then were administered sijunzi decoction, the levels of AchE, NOS, NO in the brain tissue were tested. The result showed that the levels of AchE in spleen deficiency group were obviously higher than that of control group and sijunzi decoction group (P<0.01, P<0.05), and NOS, NO was significantly lower than that of another two groups (P<0.05). Conclusions: Spleen deficiency depresses rats' ability of learning and memory.

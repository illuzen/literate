ji ning 薺寧  (in English and Chinese 中英文)

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu 朱興國編譯

 

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 Radix Moslae grosseserratae

Botanical name:

學名﹕

 Mosla grosseserrata Maxim.

Orthodon grosseserratus （Maxim.） Kudo

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

  

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

  

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

  

Other Names:

別名﹕

 臭蘇、青白蘇。

Common Name:

外文名﹕

  

Distribution:

產地﹕

 分佈於吉林、遼寧、山東、江蘇和安徽等地。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 acrid, warm.

辛，溫。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕
Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 for treatment of asthma

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

 Indications: ascites and edema; diarrhea; hyper gastric acidity; parasites with abdominal pain; swelling of hemorrhoids.

利水消腫；和胃制酸。主治腹水水腫；泄瀉；胃酸過多；虫積腹痛；痔瘡腫痛。

Chemical ingredients: [2]

化學成份﹕【2】

  
Stem and leaves contain 0.3%-0.5% of oil, mainly are : 莖葉含油率為0.3%-0.5%, 主要為：

thymol

百里香酚

carvacrol

香荊芥酚

phellandrene

水芹烯

thymoquinone

百里香醌
Whole herb contains 0.11%-0.17% of oil, mainly are: 全草含油率為0.11%-0.17%，主要成份為：

bisabolene

左旋甜沒藥烯

α-caryophyllene

α-丁 香烯

methyleugenol

甲基丁香油酚

Dosage:

用 量﹕

 Oral: 9 -15 g for decoction.

External application: appropriate amount. Smash and apply.

內服，煎湯：9~15 克。 

外用：適量，搗敷。

處方舉 例﹕

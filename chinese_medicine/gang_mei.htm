gang mei gen  崗梅根

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

藥名﹕

 Radix Cum Stem Ilicic asprellae

Biological name:

學名﹕

Ilex asprella Champ

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音：

kobaikon

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音：

Pron. in Cantonese

粤語 發音：

gong1 mui4 gan1

Other Names:

 deng cheng hua 燈稱花，wu ji gu 烏雞骨，wan dian jin 萬點金， tu gan cao 土甘草，cheng gan gen 秤杆根，ting cheng gen 汀秤根，ting cheng zi tou 汀秤仔頭，bai gan cao 白甘草，mei ye dong qing 梅葉冬青。.

Common Name:

root of rough hair holly

Distribution:

Guangdong, Guangxi, Hunan, Jiangxi provinces of China and in hill slopes of whole island of Taiwan.

廣西，廣東，湖南，江西等地及台灣全島山麓。

Properties (characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

bitter, slightly sweet, cold.

味苦，微甘。性涼。

Channels meridians) entered:歸經﹕

liver, kidney.

肝，腎經。

Actions & Indications:

用法﹕

 

Influenza with fever, thirty, swollen throat, pnuemonia, suppurative Lung disease, cough due to deficiency, chronic consumption type of infection, injuries from impact, gout, arthritis, gonorrhea, boils.

感冒發熱﹐口渴，咽喉腫痛﹔肺炎，肺癰，虛勞咳嗽，濕熱津枯，跌打損傷，風痛，風濕病， 淋病，腫毒。

Medical Function:

藥理: [1]

  1. Antibiotic effect: in vitro studies show that it possesses inhibiting effect on Staphylococcus aureus and Hemolytic streptococcus and some inhibiting effect on β-Hemolytic streptococcus.
  2. Effect on coronary blood flow: injecting with gang mei injectiones (water solution that has been treated with alcohol and NaHSO3) into in vitro heart of guinea pigs showed that the coronary blood flow increase and the contraction of the heart muscle improved. One minute after injection,  the flow amount increased 146%, and contraction of the heart muscle increase 103%.
  3. Protective effect on myocardial ischemia: it can protect myocardial ischemia of rabbits caused by posterior pituitary. It possesses certain therapeutic value on coronary diseases and angina.

 

  1. 抗菌作用： 體外試驗表明，本品對金黃色葡萄球菌、溶血性鏈球菌有抑制作用， 對乙型溶血性鏈球菌有輕度抑制作用。
  2. 對心臟冠脈流量的影響： 用離體豚鼠心臟灌流崗梅注射液（以乙醇、NaHSO3處理後之水溶液）有增加冠脈流量、加強心收縮力的作用。注 射藥物後1分鐘，流量增加146％，心收縮力增加103％。
  3. 抗心肌缺血的作用： 本品對垂體後葉素所致家兔急性心肌缺血T波改變有保護作 用，對S-T段偏移及節律紊亂亦有一定減少，對冠心病、心絞痛有一定療效。

Chemical Ingredients:

化學成份:

The roots contain triterpenoid saponins, coumarin and small amount of alkaloid. The leaves contain 0.03% of ursolic acid,（C3OH48O3。）

根據廣東韶關 地區藥檢所化學預試表明根含三萜皂甙、內酯（或香豆精），少量生物鹼。葉含0.03%的烏蘇酸 (烏索酸 亦名熊果酸、烏蘇酸，（C3OH48O3。）。

Dosage per day:

每日劑量﹕

10 to 30 g. Concentrated extract: 0.5 t 2 g.

10～30克 ；濃縮中藥：0.5～2公 克。

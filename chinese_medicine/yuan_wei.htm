yuan wei 鳶尾

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

A manuscript... writing and translation in progress

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 Rhizoma iridis tectori

Botanical name:

學名﹕

 lris tectorum Maxim

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

 

Other Names:

別名﹕

 lan hu die 藍蝴蝶，tu zhi mu 土知母，tie bian dan 鐵扁擔，shan ba cao 扇把草。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

 wall iris,  roof iris.

Distribution:

產地﹕

 originally in Japan and China, mainly in central, southwester and eastern China.

原產于我國及日本。主要分布在中原，西南和華東一帶。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 bitter, cold

苦，寒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

 for swollen throat caused by infection, abundance of phlegm, cough, and asthma.

清熱解毒，消痰，利咽。用于熱毒痰火郁結，咽喉腫痛，痰涎壅盛，咳嗽氣喘。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

 

Dosage:

用量﹕

 3～9g。

Samples of formulae

:處方舉例﹕

 

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

 

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

 The whole plant is toxic. It can cause inflammation of liver and digestive tract, and also can cause vomiting and diarrhea.

易引起消化道及肝臟發炎，並有嘔吐，下瀉等症狀。全株有毒。

skin fungus lotion 擦癬藥水

Pinyin Name: caxuan yaoshui

Translated and edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Application: for arresting pain, stopping itchiness, to be used in

Composition: ban mao 15 g,  zi jing pi 30 g,  huai jiao 30 g, bai bu 60 g,  da feng zi (de-shell) 30 g.

Add appropriate amount of alcohol. Soak for 7 days. Strain. Let stand for 3 more days. Get the top portion of clear liquid.. Adjust the total amount of liquid to 2200 ml. Make into smaller bottles of 20 ml each.

Shake well and apply to affected areas.

Warning: not to be taken orally. In cold environment, this product may show white crystal sediment. When placing the product in temperature above 15 ℃, the crystals will dissolve.  Then shake well before use.

Seal tight for storing.

Source: Medical Product Inspection Office of City of Tianjin, China.

擦癬藥水

拼音名：Caxuan Yaoshui
英文名：
書頁號：Z19-249 標准編號：WS3－B－3730－98
【處方】斑 蝥 15g， 紫荊皮 30g， 花椒 30g，百部 60g 大楓子(去殼) 30g
【制法】以上五味，加乙醇適量，浸漬七天，濾過，濾液靜置三天，吸取上清液，調整總量
至2200ml，灌裝，即得。
【性狀】本品為淺棕色液體，久置可有少量沉澱。
【檢查】應符合酊劑項下有關的各項規定(附錄Ⅰ N)。
【功能與主治】祛風，解毒，止痒。用於風癬，濕癬，金錢癬，牛皮癬等。
【用法與用量】搖勻擦抹患處。
【注意】外用藥，切勿入口。本品遇冷有白色結晶析出，可在15℃放置片刻待溶解後再用。
【規格】每瓶裝20ml
【貯藏】密封。
天津市藥品檢驗所 起草

ba qia yin 菝葜飲  (in Chinese and English 中英文)
 

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

 

ba qia yin (drink) 《source: Zhong Yao Da Ci Dian (Chinese Herb Great Dictionary 》

For: condyloma acuminata (genital wart) with infection

 

Prescription:

ba qi root 500 g, gan cao  25 g

Make into decoction by boiling twice. Mix the 2 strained extract and simmer down to 100 cc. Use 50 cc each time, twice a day.

 

菝葜飲《《 方源：中藥大辭典  》  
組成： 菝葜根500 g 甘草25 g
用法 水煎2次，濾液合並，再以文火濃縮至100mL，每服50mL，每日2次。
適應症﹕ 尖銳濕熱疣中醫辨證屬毒熱者。

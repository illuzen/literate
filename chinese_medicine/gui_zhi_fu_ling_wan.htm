gui zhi fu ling wan         (in English and Chinese 中英文)

Edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

　

　

Name of Formula: gui zhi fu ling wan

　

Application: irregular menses, abdomen pain due to blood stasis, dead fetus.

　

Composition:

gui zhi 6 g﹐fu ling 6 g﹐mo dan pi 6 g﹐tao ren 6 g﹐chi shao 6 g.

Make into decoction. One prescription per day. Make into 2 servings.

　

藥方名： 桂枝茯苓丸

功效﹕活血，化瘀，消徵

主治﹕婦女月經不調，瘀血阻滯腹痛，以及胎死腹中等症。

組成 ﹕桂 枝 6 g﹐茯 苓 6 g﹐牡丹皮 6 g﹐桃仁 6 g﹐赤芍 6 g。

水煎服。每日一劑。日服二次。
